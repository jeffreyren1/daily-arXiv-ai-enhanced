*Lindell M. Williams,Grant M. Brodnik,Scott B. Papp*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 本文探索了一种基于二次谐波（SH）稳定的新方法，用于提高光学腔中激光频率的稳定性。该方法通过检测超高Q光子谐振器的倍频间距模式，将光学频率漂移映射为微波信号（fSH），从而实现对激光频率绝对漂移的测量。实验验证了该方法的准确性，并评估了其噪声特性和灵敏度，表明SH稳定技术有望提升紧凑型精密频率参考的长期稳定性。

Method: 作者使用两台1550 nm激光器通过PDH技术检测超高Q光子谐振器的倍频间距模式（1550 nm和775 nm），将激光频率漂移映射到微波信号fSH上。并通过与外环光学频率测量对比，验证fSH的准确性。

Conclusion: 二次谐波稳定技术能够有效推断绝对频率漂移，为未来紧凑型精密频率参考的稳定性提升提供了新途径。

Abstract: The resonant modes of optical cavities provide a powerful resource for laser-frequency stabilization, underpinning high-precision metrology and coherent signal generation. We evaluate the sensitivity and noise floor of this technique, considering contributions from laser locking and bulk material properties, and conclude that $f_{SH}$ is sufficiently sensitive to enhance long-term laser-frequency stability with respect to the resonator. These results demonstrate SH stabilization as a useful technique that infers absolute drift, thereby enabling the increased stability of future compact, precision frequency references.
