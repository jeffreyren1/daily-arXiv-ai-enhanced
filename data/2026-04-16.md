### [1] [Manipulation of Superposed Vortex States of $γ$ Photon via Nonlinear Compton Scattering](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13724v1)
*Jun-Lin Zhou,Mamutjan Ababekri,Yong-Zheng Ren,Yu Wang,Ren-Tong Guo,Zhao-Hui Chen,Yu-Han Kou,Zhong-Peng Li,Jian-Xing Li*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 作者提出了一种通过非线性康普顿散射方法在γ射线区域生成可控叠加态涡旋光子的新方法，利用多频圆偏振激光场实现轨道角动量的精确调控。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 目前，γ射线区域的涡旋光子叠加态生成技术仍然是一个巨大挑战，而此类技术在光子核物理、高能物理和强场物理中具有重要应用。

Method: 作者开发了一个强场量子电动力学（QED）框架，基于多频圆偏振激光场驱动的非线性康普顿散射，通过能量简并的多光子路径干涉实现涡旋光子叠加态的生成，并计算辐射概率。轨道角动量分离和模式权重可通过激光强度和频率比调控。

Result: 该方法成功实现γ光子的可控叠加态生成，轨道角动量分离遵循Δℓ'=ν∓1（ν为频率比），且模式权重可通过激光强度调节。

Conclusion: 这项技术为强场QED和核光子学提供了重要工具，展示了通过激光场参数精确调控γ光子叠加态的能力。

Abstract: Vortex $γ$ photons in superposition states have important applications in photonuclear, high-energy, and strong-field physics. However, their controlled generation in the $γ$-ray regime remains a great challenge. Here, we put forward a novel method for the generation of vortex $γ$ photon in superposition states, with controllable orbital angular momentum (OAM) separation $Δ\ell^\prime$ and modal weights, via nonlinear Compton scattering driven by multifrequency circularly polarized laser fields. We develop a strong-field quantum electrodynamics (QED) framework to reveal the underlying mechanism and calculate the radiation probabilities. In our method, the superposition arises from interference between energy-degenerate multiphoton pathways carrying distinct OAM. For two-frequency fields, the OAM separation follows $Δ\ell'=ν\mp1$ (upper/lower sign for equal/opposite helicities), and modal weights are tunable by laser intensities, with $ν$ the frequency ratio. Vortex $γ$ photons in controllable superposition states from our method have significant applications in strong-field QED and nuclear photonics.

### [2] [Towards SAFE-ISAC: STAR-RIS-Aided Joint Jamming Suppression and Target Concealment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14097v1)
*Radwa Sultan*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 论文提出了一种名为SAFE-ISAC的框架，利用STAR-RIS联合抑制主动干扰和恶意检测，并通过优化STAR-RIS的反射和传输响应来解决非凸问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现代无线系统面临协同主动干扰和恶意检测的双重威胁，这些威胁会破坏下行通信并暴露ISAC目标的存在，因此需要设计鲁棒架构以应对这些攻击。

Method: 通过优化STAR-RIS的反射和传输响应，将非凸问题分解为两个子问题：在传输子空间中使用Dinkelbach方法和SDP松弛解决恶意检测问题；在反射子空间中通过Polak-Reibére Riemannian共轭梯度算法抑制干扰。

Result: 数值结果表明，与现有基准相比，SAFE-ISAC框架能有效实现干扰抑制和目标隐藏，同时满足通信和传感的QoS需求。

Conclusion: SAFE-ISAC框架为解决无线系统中的协同干扰和恶意检测问题提供了有效解决方案，显著提升了系统的安全性。

Abstract: Designing robust architectures that can mitigate sophisticated attacks is now a key priority for modern wireless systems. This paper investigates a single-cell bistatic integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) network facing simultaneous coordinated active jamming and malicious detection. These threats aim to disrupt the downlink communication and detect the presence of the ISAC target, respectively. To counter these attacks, we propose the SAFE-ISAC framework, which utilizes a simultaneous transmit and reflect reconfigurable intelligent surface (STAR-RIS) to jointly suppress jamming power and reduce the malicious detector's Signal-to-Interference-plus-Noise Ratio (SINR). We formulate a joint minimization problem for jamming gain and detection probability by optimizing the STAR-RIS reflection and transmission responses. This non-convex problem is decoupled into two subproblems: i) malicious detection mitigation in the transmission subspace, solved using the Dinkelbach method and Semidefinite Programming (SDP) relaxation, and ii) jamming suppression in the reflection subspace, addressed via Polak-Reibére Riemannian conjugate gradient algorithm. Numerical results validate that the proposed scheme effectively achieves jamming mitigation and target concealment while meeting all communication and sensing Quality-of-Service (QoS) requirements, compared to existing benchmarks.
