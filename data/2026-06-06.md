### [1] [Instantaneous Risk Minimization for Secure Integrated Sensing and Communication](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03372v2)
*Chao Ge,Na Zhao,Yuan Shen*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种用于安全集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统的鲁棒波束成形框架，旨在最小化瞬时信息泄漏风险，而非传统设计中最大化遍历保密率的思路。通过多目标优化问题联合抑制最坏情况下窃听者的信干噪比（SINR）、提升感知精度并保障合法用户的服务质量。采用分层迭代算法求解该非凸问题，理论分析和仿真结果表明该方法在保证每传输安全性的同时具有实用复杂性。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的ISAC系统设计通常关注最大化遍历保密率，但在实际场景中，瞬时信息泄漏风险对安全性至关重要。本文针对这一局限，提出了一种新的鲁棒波束成形框架，旨在最小化信息泄漏风险，确保物理层的安全性。

Method: 本文提出了一个多目标优化问题，联合优化最坏情况下窃听者的SINR抑制、感知精度提升和合法用户的QoS保障。为了解决这一非凸问题，设计了一种分层迭代算法：外环基于更新的感知性能细化连续不确定性区域，内环在细化后的不确定性区域下优化波束成形。

Result: 理论分析和仿真结果表明，所提出的方法能够实现每传输的安全性保障，并且算法的复杂度在实际应用中是可接受的。

Conclusion: 本文提出的鲁棒波束成形框架能够在ISAC系统中有效降低瞬时信息泄漏风险，同时兼顾感知精度和用户服务质量，为物理层安全提供了新的解决方案。

Abstract: To ensure worst-case physical layer security, this paper proposes a robust beamforming framework for secure integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems. Different from conventional designs that focus on maximizing the ergodic secrecy rate, the proposed method aims to minimize instantaneous information leakage risk. We formulate a multi-objective optimization problem that jointly suppresses the worst-case eavesdropper signal-to-interference-plus-noise ratio (SINR), improving sensing accuracy, and ensuring the quality of service (QoS) for legitimate users. To address the resulting non-convex problem, we develop a hierarchical iterative algorithm, in which the outer loop refines the continuous uncertainty regions based on the updated sensing performance, and the inner loop optimizes beamforming under the refined uncertainty regions. Theoretical analysis and simulation results demonstrate that the proposed method achieves per-transmission security guarantees with practical complexity.

### [2] [Adaptive $c_2$-Perturbed AFDM Waveform Design for Integrated Sensing and Communication](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04698v1)
*Shiqi Cui,Fan Zhang,Yuanshuo Gang,Zeping Sui,Tianqi Mao,Zhaocheng Wang*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种实时数据驱动框架，通过优化预啁啾参数 $c_2$ 来解决AFDM在ISAC系统中的高峰均比（PAPR）和不完美的自相关旁瓣问题，从而提升系统性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: AFDM（仿射频分复用）在时间-频率双色散信道中表现出色，是集成传感与通信（ISAC）系统的理想波形选择。然而，AFDM仍面临高峰均比（PAPR）和随机数据符号导致的自相关旁瓣不完美的问题，这些问题限制了其性能。本文旨在通过优化预啁啾参数 $c_2$ 来解决这些挑战，从而提升AFDM-ISAC系统的整体表现。

Method: 1. 提出了一种实时数据驱动框架，用于优化AFDM系统的预啁啾参数 $c_2$。2. 设计了一个无需侧信息的优化问题，目标是降低PAPR，并减少非周期和周期自相关函数的加权积分旁瓣水平。3. 开发了一种高效的非单调线搜索谱投影梯度算法，通过利用闭式梯度降低计算复杂度。这一方法的复杂度与传统AFDM接收器相当。

Result: 仿真结果表明：1. 所提方法在PAPR和自相关旁瓣性能方面均有显著提升；2. 在功率放大器非线性较严重的情况下，能够实现更好的误码率性能；3. 在通信与传感性能之间实现了优异的权衡。

Conclusion: 通过优化预啁啾参数 $c_2$，本文提出的数据驱动框架有效解决了AFDM在ISAC系统中的PAPR和自相关旁瓣问题。该方法不仅提升了系统性能，还保持了与传统AFDM接收器相当的计算复杂度，为AFDM-ISAC系统的实际应用提供了可行方案。

Abstract: Affine frequency division multiplexing (AFDM) is a promising waveform for integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems owing to its superior performance in time--frequency doubly dispersive channels. However, AFDM still faces a pair of challenges: high PAPR and random data symbols produce imperfect autocorrelation sidelobes. To address these challenges, this paper proposes a real-time data-driven framework that optimizes the pre-chirp parameter $c_2$ to enhance the AFDM-ISAC performance. Specifically, a side-information-free optimization problem is formulated to reduce PAPR and the weighted integrated sidelobe levels of both aperiodic and periodic autocorrelation functions, with complexity comparable to that of the conventional AFDM receiver. Furthermore, an efficient non-monotone line-search spectral projected-gradient algorithm is developed by exploiting closed-form gradients. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed method achieves a superior sensing vs. communications trade-off and is capable of striking a promoted bit error rate performance in the presence of severe power amplifier nonlinearity.

### [3] [Foundation Models for Wireless Communications: From PHY Intelligence to Network Autonomy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06239v1)
*Le Liang,Jiajia Guo,Jun Zhang,Chan-Byoung Chae,Lu Lu,Shugong Xu,Octavia A. Dobre,Shi Jin,Geoffrey Ye Li*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文探讨了基于基础模型的AI解决方案如何重塑6G网络的物理层处理与无线资源管理，覆盖了从现成预训练模型到无线原生模型和智能代理模型的三个演进范式，并讨论了其在6G新兴领域中的应用与挑战。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 6G网络将引入前所未有的复杂性，需要一种新的网络优化与管理范式。基于AI的解决方案，尤其是借助基础模型的能力，成为解决这一问题的潜在途径。基础模型具备通用特征提取能力，能够通过少量或零样本调适应用于广泛的无线任务。

Method: 文章提出三个渐进的范式：1) 利用现成预训练基础模型适应无线任务；2) 开发无线原生基础模型，从无线数据中训练，以捕捉跨域模态差异和通用无线物理特征；3) 引入智能代理基础模型，将静态数据处理提升为自主推理驱动的网络编排。

Result: 研究表明，基础模型在6G新兴领域（如ISAC、新型MIMO架构、语义通信和系统级网络自治）中展现出巨大潜力。然而，实现完全智能和自适应的无线网络仍需克服多项挑战。

Conclusion: 基础模型为6G网络提供了全新的优化与管理思路，尤其是在物理层和资源管理方面。尽管面临挑战，但通过进一步研究，有望实现完全智能化的无线网络未来。

Abstract: 6G networks will introduce unprecedented complexity, which calls for a paradigm shift in network optimization and management. Artificial intelligence (AI)-based solutions, especially those enabled by the recently developed foundation models, have been recognized as promising candidates. Foundation models are large-scale AI models with general-purpose feature extraction capabilities, and once trained on massive amounts of data, they can be adapted to solve a wide range of downstream tasks, either in a zero-shot manner or with few-shot fine-tuning. This article provides a comprehensive overview of how foundation models are reshaping physical-layer processing and wireless resource management across three progressive paradigms. First, we examine the adaptation of off-the-shelf pre-trained foundation models to various wireless tasks. Second, we explore wireless-native foundation models, built from scratch on wireless data to bridge cross-domain modality gaps and capture universal wireless-domain physical characteristics. Third, we highlight agentic foundation models, which elevate static data processing into autonomous, reasoning-driven network orchestration. Furthermore, we discuss the impact of applying foundation models to emerging 6G frontiers, including integrated sensing and communications (ISAC), new multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) architectures, semantic communications, and system-level network autonomy. Finally, we identify critical open challenges and opportunities, charting a promising path toward fully intelligent and adaptive wireless networks.
