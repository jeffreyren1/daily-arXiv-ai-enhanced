### [1] [Statistical Analysis of using the Shapley Value for Sensor Anomaly Localization with Accurate Classifiers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.00867v1)
*Xubin Fang,Rick S. Blum*

Main category: stat.ML

TL;DR: 本文研究了基于Shapley值的传感器异常/攻击定位方法的性能，证明了在某些独立观测情况下，基于Shapley值的优化异常测试与简化版测试（仅使用Shapley值计算中的一个项）具有相同的错误概率；而在某些依赖观测情况下（如双变量高斯/拉普拉斯分布），两种测试不同且可能表现优劣不一，提出结合两种方法可获得更优结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 近年来，许多研究提出使用Shapley值进行传感器异常或攻击的定位，但缺乏对这种方法的理论统计性能分析。作者旨在填补这一空白，探讨基于Shapley值的定位方法的有效性，特别是在独立和依赖观测情况下的表现差异。

Method: 作者通过数学定义的二元分类器（优化异常测试）来分析Shapley值在传感器异常检测中的性能。对于独立观测情况，证明了优化Shapley值测试与简化测试等价；对于依赖观测情况（如双变量高斯/拉普拉斯分布），研究了两种测试的决策区域和错误概率差异，并探讨了其优劣条件。此外，提出了结合两种方法的改进策略。

Result: 研究发现，在独立观测情况下，优化Shapley值测试与简化测试表现相同；而在依赖观测情况下，两种测试可能表现不同，优劣取决于相关性的符号和攻击类型。在某些高相关性的双变量高斯场景中，优化Shapley值测试可能劣于简化测试，而在其他情况下可能更优。结合两种方法可以进一步提升性能。数值实验验证了这些结论。

Conclusion: 本文首次对基于Shapley值的传感器异常定位方法进行了理论统计性能分析，揭示了其在独立和依赖观测情况下的表现差异，并证明了在某些依赖情况下，结合两种测试方法可以取得更优结果。这些发现为Shapley值的应用提供了新的理论支持，并鼓励后续研究进一步优化和改进这一方法。

Abstract: Recent publications have suggested using the Shap- ley value for sensor anomaly/attack localization. We study the performance of such an approach by using mathematically de- fined optimum binary classifiers in the Shapley value calculation. To judge localization performance, we study the ability of the Shapley value of a given sensor observation to determine if that observation is anomalous. First, we prove that for cases with independent sensor observations, an optimized anomaly test using the Shapley value is equivalent to an optimized lower-complexity anomaly test using a single term in the Shapley value calculation, yielding the exact same probability of error. For some popular dependent observation cases involving two sensors, including correlated bivariate Gaussian/Laplacian probability density functions and constant/Gaussian at- tacks/anomalies, we prove that these two tests are fundamentally different, yielding different decision regions and error probabil- ities. Further, we prove that the Shapley value test is sometimes strictly inferior to the other (single term in Shapley calculation) test in certain statistically dependent bivariate Gaussian scenarios with large correlation magnitude and additive attacks/anomalies, while it is strictly superior in others, depending on the sign of the correlation. One can combine these two approaches to obtain a strictly better approach in these cases. These results, which provide the first theoretical statistical analysis of Shapley-based localization, seem very interesting based on the wide acceptance of the Shapley value by many researchers and should encourage further research on this topic. Numerical results are provided which illustrate our findings.

### [2] [Integrated Sensing and Covert Communication In Low-Altitude Networks: A Smart Radio Environment Perspective](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02077v1)

Conclusion: 本文证明了SRE技术在优化隐蔽通信和感知集成中的潜力，并为未来的低空网络通信安全提供了新的研究方向。

Abstract: The rise of low-altitude economies and 6G is driving the evolution of low-altitude networks (LANs), making communication security a pressing concern. We first survey the applications and state-of-the-art findings of ISACC in LANs, highlighting key practical challenges. Subsequently, we introduce the core concept of SRE and elaborate on its enabling techniques across four dimensions. To deliver more insights, we explore potential pathways for integrating SRE into ISACC. To maximize covert throughput, a reinforcement learning-based case study is conducted by jointly optimizing flight trajectory, jamming power, movable antenna position, bandwidth allocation, and beamforming vectors. Simulation results show that the proposed scheme achieves superior performance compared to the benchmark. Finally, some open challenges and potential directions are discussed.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 集成传感与通信（ISAC）系统虽然能同时实现传感和数据传输，但存在探测信号可能被拦截的漏洞，暴露了传感行为和信息。现有物理层安全方法虽能缓解拦截问题，但信号仍可被被动监视者检测到，未能完全解决隐蔽性问题。

Method: 作者提出了SNF-PRP（亚噪声地板伪随机探测）框架，基于KL散度提供隐蔽性保证，利用宽频带分析中的扩展增益，并以闭合形式推导出实现目标Cramér-Rao界限（CRB）所需的最小积分长度。

Result: 在5G NR n78参数下的模拟实验中，该框架实现了0.5米范围内的距离测量和0.5米/秒的速度测量精度，KL散度低于隐蔽性阈值的5.8倍，证明了在低探测功率（-12 dB和-15 dB）下的联合可行性。
