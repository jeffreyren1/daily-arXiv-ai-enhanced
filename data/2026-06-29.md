### [1] [Delayed Verification Destabilizes Multi-Agent LLM Belief: Instability Thresholds and Optimal Corrector Placement](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27409v1)
*Igor Itkin*

Main category: eess.SY

TL;DR: 该论文研究了多智能体大型语言模型系统中因验证延迟导致的虚假信息传播问题，提出了基于图上的接地修正器节点的延迟共识模型，并通过谱分解发现验证剂量的稳定性阈值。同时，提出了一个超模分配目标和贪婪算法来优化修正器节点的分配。实验验证了理论预测的震荡效应，并指出接地事实回答可以消除这种不稳定性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多智能体大型语言模型（LLM）系统通常依赖验证器和批评器智能体来抑制幻觉现象，但由于验证延迟的存在，虚假声明可能在延迟期间在网络中传播。作者试图解决这一问题，通过建模延迟共识过程，分析验证延迟对系统稳定性的影响。

Method: 作者提出了一种基于图论的模型，将延迟共识建模为带有接地修正器节点的图。通过接地拉普拉斯矩阵的谱分解，得到了验证剂量的封闭形式稳定性阈值。此外，提出了一个超模分配目标和一个贪婪近似算法，用于在有限的修正器预算下优化节点分配。实验部分验证了理论预测的震荡效应。

Result: 实验结果表明，当通信延迟与验证延迟一致时，系统最不稳定；对于延迟为2的情况，稳定性阈值是逆黄金比例。同时，接地事实回答能够消除这种不稳定性，表明不稳定性仅存在于带符号信念任务中。

Conclusion: 论文的结论是验证延迟和验证剂量的相互作用会影响多智能体LLM系统的稳定性，尤其是当通信和验证延迟一致时。通过接地修正器节点的合理分配和接地事实回答的设计，可以显著减少虚假信息的传播。这种不稳定性是带符号信念任务的特定现象，而接地验证则始终具有稳定作用。

Abstract: Multi-agent large language model (LLM) systems often rely on verifier and critic agents to suppress hallucinations, but verification is delayed. During this delay, false claims can propagate through the agent network. We model this process as delayed consensus on a graph with grounded corrector nodes. Spectral decomposition by the grounded Laplacian yields a closed-form stability threshold for the verification dose: correction that is too strong or too delayed can turn consensus into oscillation. The most unstable regime occurs when the communication and verification delays coincide; for delay two, the threshold is the inverse golden ratio. The same framework gives a supermodular placement objective and a greedy (1-1/e)-approximation rule for assigning a limited corrector budget to influential nodes. Experiments across five open models confirm the predicted dose-delay oscillations. By contrast, grounded factual answering makes truth an absorbing boundary and eliminates the effect, suggesting that the instability is specific to signed-belief tasks while grounded verification remains stabilizing

### [2] [Unlocking Cryogenic Energy Storage by Constructing Dipole Glass with Unit-cell-level Polar Disorder](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27887v1)
*Yangyang Si,Denan Li,Yijie Li,Changsheng Chen,Jingxuan Li,Chao Zhou,Hao Xiong,Tianfu Zhang,Wenjin Liao,Zhongqi Ren,Huaicheng Yuan,Dong Li,Jing-Kai Qin,Cheng-Yan Xu,Ye Zhu,Yunlong Tang,Sujit Das,Jieun Kim,Junling Wang,Hao Pan,Fei Li,Zhen Chen,Shi Liu,Zuhuang Chen*

Main category: cond-mat.mtrl-sci

TL;DR: 该论文通过在Pb0.6Sr0.4ZrO3薄膜中设计单元级别的无序偶极玻璃态，实现了超低温下的高效能量存储，解决了传统电化学系统和弛豫基介电电容器在低温下的局限性。该方法在4K温度下实现了超高能量密度（211 J/cm³）和超低滞后损耗（效率> 88%），并在10⁸次充放电循环中表现稳定。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 低温能量存储对深空探测和量子计算等前沿技术至关重要，但传统电化学系统在低于230K时因离子迁移冻结而失效，而弛豫基介电电容器在超低温下因极性纳米域冻结导致滞后损耗增加，限制了其应用。因此，需要开发一种在超低温下高效的能量存储系统。

Method: 通过在接近反铁电-顺电相边界的Pb0.6Sr0.4ZrO3薄膜中设计单元级的无序偶极玻璃态，抑制了长程铁电有序性。这种偶极玻璃态增强了偶极相互作用的复杂性，从而在超低温下实现了低滞后和高能量密度的性能。

Result: 该方法在4K温度下实现了88%以上的效率，能量密度高达211 J/cm³，并且在9 MV/cm电场下稳定运行，具备10⁸次充放电循环的耐久性和微秒级充放电能力。

Conclusion: 该研究为超低温介电电容器建立了一种偶极玻璃态范式，为高效能量存储系统开辟了新途径，有望在前沿纳米电子学中广泛应用。与其他研究相比，该论文的突破在于通过偶极玻璃态设计解决了低温下滞后损耗和能量密度下降的问题，显著提升了低温能量存储的性能和稳定性。

Abstract: Cryogenic energy storage is vital for frontier technologies including deep-space exploration and quantum computing, yet conventional electrochemical energy systems fail below ~230 K due to frozen ion migration. While relaxor-based dielectric capacitors provide high efficiency at room temperature, the intrinsic freezing/growth of polar nanodomains at extended cryogenic regime limits their applications with deteriorated hysteresis losses. Here, we realize superior cryogenic energy-storage performance by designing unit-cell-level disordered dipole-glass state in Pb0.6Sr0.4ZrO3 thin films with composition near antiferroelectric-paraelectric phase boundary. The antiferroelectric-derived dipole-glass introduces enhanced unit-cell-level complexity of dipole interaction that suppresses long-range ferroelectric order. This enables ultralow-hysteresis operation (efficiency > 88%) down to 4 K, delivering record-high energy density (211 J/cm^3) at 9 MV/cm, stability over 10^8 charge/discharge cycles and microsecond-scale charge/discharge capability. This work establishes a dipole-glass paradigm for cryogenic dielectric capacitors, opening a new avenue to highly-efficient energy-storage systems with broad applications in frontier nanoelectronics.

</details>
