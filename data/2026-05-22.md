### [1] [Rate-Splitting--Inspired Bistatic OFDM-ISAC](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21729v1)
*Bruno F. Costa,Anup Mishra,Israel Leyva-Mayorga,Taufik Abrão,Petar Popovski*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文针对OFDM波形下的上行双基地ISAC系统中由于直接路径与回波路径贡献不均以及多普勒引起的ICI导致的干扰问题，提出了一种基于RS（资源分割）的框架，将通信消息分为稳健流和补充流，并与感知信号联合叠加，设计了一种分阶段的感知-通信接收机。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在OFDM波形下的上行双基地ISAC系统中，直接路径与回波路径的不均衡贡献以及多普勒引起的ICI（子载波间干扰）导致干扰结构复杂，传统的正交资源分离和固定SIC策略无法有效解决这一问题。因此，作者试图提出一种新的框架来高效管理干扰，提升系统性能。

Method: 作者提出了一种基于RS（资源分割）的框架，将通信消息分为稳健流和补充流，并与感知信号联合叠加。此外，设计了一种分阶段的感知-通信接收机。通过推导每个子载波的SINR表达式并基于Fisher信息建立感知精度与通信可靠性的关系，作者将问题建模为一个在感知性能和功率约束下的联合功率分配问题，并用凸代理和分数规划方法解决了这一非凸问题。

Result: 数值结果表明，与基于NOMA的基线方法相比，该框架能够更有效地管理内部干扰（IFI）并提高对多普勒引起的ICI的鲁棒性。

Conclusion: 本文提出的基于RS的框架为OFDM波形下的上行双基地ISAC系统提供了一种有效的干扰管理方法，显著提升了系统的频谱效率和鲁棒性。

Abstract: Achieving effective uplink bistatic ISAC over an OFDM waveform gives rise to challenging interference structures. These are mostly due to unequal direct- and echo-path contributions and Doppler-induced ICI, rendering orthogonal resource separation and fixed SIC strategies inadequate. To address this problem, we propose a RS-inspired framework where the transmitter splits each communication message into a robust and a supplementary stream, which are jointly superposed over a sensing signal. Furthermore, we present the design of a staged sensing-communication receiver. Based on this framework, we derive tractable per-subcarrier SINR expressions and establish the relation between sensing accuracy and communication reliability based on the Fisher information. Building on these, we formulate a joint power-allocation problem for SE maximization under sensing-performance and power constraints. The resulting non-convex formulation is solved using convex surrogates and fractional programming. Numerical results demonstrate that, compared to NOMA-inspired baselines, the proposed framework provides more effective IFI management and improved robustness to Doppler-induced ICI.

### [2] [UAV-based Energy-Efficient Data Collection in Smart Grids with ISAC QoS Guarantees](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22490v1)
*Yibin Xie,Jin Zhao,Indrakshi Dey,Nicola Marchetti*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于无人机协同数据收集框架，结合集成感知与通信（ISAC）技术，以解决动态线路评级（DLR）中因监测设备连接中断导致的数据缺失或延迟问题。通过联合优化无人机轨迹和数据收集调度，显著降低了能耗。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 动态线路评级（DLR）需要实时监测数据以确定电力线路的实时容量，但监测设备（MD）易受连接中断影响，导致数据缺失或延迟。无人机（UAV）虽然能增强数据收集的鲁棒性，但其有限的机载能量和飞行风险限制了在长距离输电走廊中及时监测的能力。

Method: 提出了一种基于无人机协同的数据收集框架，结合集成感知与通信（ISAC）技术，以确保安全和协同的数据收集。通过联合能量最小化问题优化无人机轨迹和数据收集调度，并提出了结合深度强化学习（DRL）和半定松驰（SDR）的混合算法来解决非凸的ISAC约束问题。

Result: 仿真结果表明，与离线基准相比，所提方案能耗降低高达34.6%；与分离的感知与通信基线相比，能耗降低约2.2%，同时满足DLR的分钟级时间尺度需求。

Conclusion: 该框架有效解决了电力线路实时监测中的数据缺失和延迟问题，同时通过优化的无人机协同和ISAC技术实现了能源效率的提升，为DLR的实际应用提供了可行的解决方案。

Abstract: Dynamic line rating (DLR) is a methodology that requires timely monitoring data to determine the real-time ampacity of power lines. However, DLR monitoring devices (MD) are vulnerable to connectivity disruptions, leading to missing or delayed data. Although unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV) can enable resilient data collection from MD, their limited onboard energy challenges timely monitoring over extended transmission corridors with flight hazards. This paper proposes a cooperative UAV-based data collection framework with integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) to support timely DLR updates. In this framework, ISAC is employed to maintain the sensing and communication quality required for safe and cooperative UAV data collection. Accordingly, a joint energy minimization problem is formulated over UAV trajectories and collection scheduling under ISAC constraints. To solve it, a hybrid algorithm combining deep reinforcement learning (DRL) and semidefinite relaxation (SDR) is proposed, where DRL optimizes the trajectory and collection scheduling, while SDR is used to handle the non-convex ISAC constraints. Simulation results show that the proposed scheme reduces energy consumption by up to 34.6% compared with offline benchmarks and by about 2.2% compared with the separated sensing-and-communication baseline, while satisfying the minute-level timescale requirement of DLR.

### [3] [Chiral Electromagnetic Surface Waves on Chern-Simons Interfaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21577v1)
*Nemanja Kaloper*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究了在共维度-1的Chern-Simons界面上存在的电磁表面波，发现这种界面支持一种手性表面光子模式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 作者试图解决的问题是：在传统的真空电动力学中，是否存在一种机制可以在没有环境材料响应或几何约束的情况下支持手性电磁表面波。

Method: 通过解析边值问题，作者研究了Chern-Simons界面效应对电磁波传播的影响。具体分析了界面两侧的电磁波行为，并展示了Chern-Simons相互作用如何对不同螺旋度的电磁波产生相反的影响。

Result: 研究发现，即使在没有材料响应或几何限制的情况下，Chern-Simons界面也能支持一种局域化的手性表面光子模式。这种模式具有线性色散关系和由Chern-Simons系数决定的频率无关折射率。

Conclusion: 论文得出结论，Chern-Simons界面为电磁表面波提供了一种新的传播机制，突破了传统理论对材料响应或几何约束的依赖。

Abstract: We show that Maxwell theory with a codimension-$1$ Chern-Simons interface supports chiral electromagnetic surface waves on the interface, even when the bulk theory on both sides is conventional vacuum electrodynamics in infinite space. Solving the exact boundary value problem we find that the Chern-Simons interaction acts with opposite sign on the two helicities oriented along the interface, giving rise to one normalizable mode localized on the interface. This mode is a gapless chiral surface photon with linear dispersion and a frequency-independent index of refraction set by the Chern-Simons coefficient. This mode exists despite the absence of ambient material response or geometric confinement.

</details>
