### [1] [Multidimensional Opinion Dynamics with Confirmation Bias: A Multi-Layer Framework](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21081v1)
*M. Hossein Abedinzadeh,Emrah Akyol*

Main category: math.DS

TL;DR: 本文研究了社会网络中受确认偏误影响的多维意见动态，通过信息对称的社会渠道和非对称的外部信息渠道建模，探讨了确认偏误对意见收敛的影响，并给出了稳态条件和计算方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在多维意见动态中，确认偏误对个体意见的影响尚未被充分研究。作者旨在建模和分析这种影响，尤其是在社会网络和外部信息渠道的共同作用下，意见如何收敛到稳态。

Method: 使用静态对称的社会渠道和动态非对称的外部信息渠道建模；通过依赖于状态的源影响函数捕捉确认偏误；分析Lipschitz源影响函数下的收敛条件；对仿射确认偏误函数，提出有限符号一致性搜索方法；对非线性源影响函数，推导不动点的上下界。

Result: 对于一般Lipschitz源影响函数，给出了动态收缩并收敛到唯一稳态的条件；对于仿射确认偏误函数，稳态可通过有限搜索计算，部分情况下有闭式解；对非线性函数，推导了不动点的显式界限。数值实验表明忽略确认偏误会改变源设计结论。

Conclusion: 多维耦合和确认偏误在多维意见动态中起关键作用；稳态分析的条件和方法具有普适性；忽略确认偏误可能导致对源设计的错误结论。

Abstract: We study multidimensional opinion dynamics under confirmation bias in social networks. Each agent holds a vector of correlated opinions across multiple topic layers. Peer interaction is modeled through a static, informationally symmetric social channel, while external information enters through a dynamic, informationally asymmetric source channel. Source influence is described by nonnegative state-dependent functions of agent--source opinion mismatch, which captures confirmation bias without hard thresholds. For general Lipschitz source-influence functions, we give sufficient conditions under which the dynamics are contractive and converge to a unique steady state independent of the initial condition. For affine confirmation-bias functions, we show that the steady state can be computed through a finite sign-consistency search and identify a regime in which it admits a closed form. For broader classes of bounded nonlinear source-influence functions, we derive explicit lower and upper bounds on the fixed point. Numerical examples and a study on a real-world adolescent lifestyle network illustrate the role of multidimensional coupling and show that source-design conclusions can change qualitatively when confirmation bias is ignored.

### [2] [Graph-based data-driven discovery of interpretable laws governing corona-induced noise and radio interference for high-voltage transmission lines](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20600v1)
*Hao Xu,Yuntian Chen,Chongqing Kang,Dongxiao Zhang*

Main category: physics.app-ph

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一个名为Mono-GraphMD的单调性约束图符号发现框架，用于预测超高压（UHV）交流输电线路的电晕噪声（AN）和无线电干扰（RI），以解决现有经验法则预测准确性不足的问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 全球向可再生能源转型需要开发超高压（UHV）输电技术以连接偏远能源与城市需求。然而，UHV电网的实施常因电晕噪声（AN）和无线电干扰（RI）而受限，这些排放需符合严格的环境标准。现有工程实践依赖的固定对数线性经验法则预测准确性不足，限制了其大规模应用。

Method: 论文提出了一种单调性约束的图符号发现框架Mono-GraphMD，用于发现电晕噪声和无线电干扰的紧凑、可解释的定律。该框架通过分析表面梯度、束数和直径之间的非线性相互作用，提供了高场排放的机理洞察，并能够准确预测电晕试验数据和实际多国UHV线路的数据。

Result: Mono-GraphMD能够准确预测16束导体的电晕试验数据和实际UHV线路的电晕噪声与无线电干扰。与黑盒模型不同，该框架发现的闭式定律具有高度的可移植性和可解释性，适用于多种场景的快速预测，从而优化工程设计过程。

Conclusion: 论文提出的Mono-GraphMD框架不仅显著提高了电晕噪声和无线电干扰的预测准确性，还提供了机理上的见解，为超高压输电线路的设计和合规性评估提供了实用工具。其可解释性和可移植性使其在实际工程中具有广泛的应用潜力。

Abstract: The global shift towards renewable energy necessitates the development of ultrahigh-voltage (UHV) AC transmission to bridge the gap between remote energy sources and urban demand. While UHV grids offer superior capacity and efficiency, their implementation is often hindered by corona-induced audible noise (AN) and radio interference (RI). Since these emissions must meet strict environmental compliance standards, accurate prediction is vital for the large-scale deployment of UHV infrastructure. Existing engineering practices often rely on empirical laws, in which fixed log-linear structures limit accuracy and extrapolation. Herein, we present a monotonicity-constrained graph symbolic discovery framework, Mono-GraphMD, which uncovers compact, interpretable laws for corona-induced AN and RI. The framework provides mechanistic insight into how nonlinear interactions among the surface gradient, bundle number and diameter govern high-field emissions and enables accurate predictions for both corona-cage data and multicountry real UHV lines with up to 16-bundle conductors. Unlike black-box models, the discovered closed-form laws are highly portable and interpretable, allowing for rapid predictions when applied to various scenarios, thereby facilitating the engineering design process.

### [3] [Secure Cell-Free Massive MIMO ISAC Systems: Joint AP Selection and Power Allocation Against Eavesdropping](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18635v1)
*Ruiguang Wang,Takumi Takahashi,Hideki Ochiai*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文研究了一种解决感知区域内信息泄露问题的无蜂窝大规模多输入多输出（CF-mMIMO）集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统，通过提出一种新颖的接入点（AP）选择策略，将AP划分为通信AP和感知AP两组，并采用连续凸近似（SCA）方法优化系统性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在CF-mMIMO ISAC系统中，潜在窃听者位于感知区域内可能导致信息泄露，这是一个关键挑战。作者旨在通过优化AP的功能划分和资源分配，提高系统的通信效率、感知精度和安全性。

Method: 作者提出了AP选择策略，将AP分为通信AP（C-APs）和感知AP（S-APs），分别专注于数据传输和目标检测与窃听抑制。通过推导通信速率、窃听速率和主瓣与平均旁瓣比（MASR）的闭式表达式评估性能，并利用连续凸近似（SCA）方法解决两个互补的优化问题：在安全约束下最大化用户速率和在满足服务质量（QoS）要求下最小化窃听速率。

Result: 数值结果验证了理论分析，显示了显著的性能提升，揭示了通信效率、感知精度和安全性之间的固有权衡。

Conclusion: 本文提出的联合优化框架为设计安全的CF-mMIMO ISAC系统提供了实用指导，展示了AP分功能分配和资源优化的有效性。

Abstract: This paper investigates a cell-free massive multiple-input-multiple-output (CF-mMIMO) integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) system that addresses the critical challenge of information leakage to potential eavesdroppers located within sensing zones. A novel access point (AP) selection strategy is proposed, which partitions the distributed APs into two functional groups: communication APs (C-APs), dedicated exclusively to data transmission, and sensing APs (S-APs), responsible for target detection and eavesdropper suppression. Closed-form expressions for the achievable communication rate, eavesdropping rate, and mainlobe-to-average-sidelobe ratio (MASR) are derived to evaluate system performance. Two complementary optimization problems are formulated using the successive convex approximation (SCA): (i) maximizing user rates under security constraints and (ii) minimizing eavesdropping rates while satisfying quality of service (QoS) requirements. The proposed joint optimization framework determines the optimal AP operational modes and power allocation across communication and sensing links. Extensive numerical results validate the theoretical analysis and demonstrate significant performance gains, revealing inherent trade-offs among communication efficiency, sensing accuracy, and security. These insights offer practical guidelines for designing secure CF-mMIMO ISAC systems.

### [4] [Multibeam Phased Arrays with Spherical Gold Spatio-temporal Coding for Fading-Resilient and Delay Robust Beam Isolations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19578v1)
*Yuan Ma,Mike Ballou,Kyle Richard,Hessam Mahdavifar,Najme Ebrahimi*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为Spherical-Gold的方案，通过将中等长度的Gold序列嵌入球形空间码本中，解决了传统时间编码多波束阵列在延迟和衰落条件下隔离度差的问题。该方法在时间误差和衰落条件下实现了至少15 dB的抑制和小于2.5 dB的旁瓣电平变化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 未来的集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统需要支持低延迟硬件和鲁棒隔离的多波束同时操作。传统的时间编码多波束阵列在延迟和衰落条件下表现出旁瓣电平（SLL）大幅下降的问题，隔离度严重不足，影响了系统性能。

Method: 本文提出了一种名为Spherical-Gold的方案，将中等长度的Gold序列嵌入球形空间码本中，利用时间和空间相关性界限，实现了波束间的高效隔离。实验验证采用了Analog Devices ADAR3002 Ka波段256元接收器和四波束同时操作。

Result: 实验结果证明，Spherical-Gold方案在时间误差和衰落条件下实现了至少15 dB的抑制和小于2.5 dB的旁瓣电平变化。相比之下，传统的时间编码CDMA方案在时间延迟下的旁瓣电平下降到约-5至-7 dB，且变化幅度接近8 dB。

Conclusion: Spherical-Gold方案通过结合时间和空间相关性，显著提高了多波束系统的隔离性能和鲁棒性，适用于未来集成感知与通信系统的低延迟和高效隔离需求。

Abstract: Future integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems require simultaneous multibeam operation with low-latency hardware and robust isolation under synchronization error and fading. Conventional code-division multiplexing using Walsh-Hadamard codes is extremely time-sensitive. This paper demonstrates that conventional temporal-only coded multibeam arrays suffer from inter-beam sidelobe level (SLL) collapse to within a few dB of the main lobe, with variations exceeding 10-20 dB over delay. By embedding moderate-length Gold sequences into a spherical spatial codebook, the proposed Spherical-Gold scheme leverages both temporal and spatial correlation bounds, achieving effective inter-beam isolation without increasing RF complexity. Measurement results and verifications are performed using an Analog Devices ADAR3002 Ka-band 256-element receiver with four simultaneous beams. The proposed scheme demonstrates at least 15 dB rejection with less than 2.5 dB variation in SLL under time error and fading, whereas temporal-only CDMA degrades to approximately -5 to -7 dB SLL with nearly 8 dB variation under time delay.

</details>
