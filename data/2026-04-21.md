### [1] [Goal-oriented Resource Allocation for Collaborative Integrated Sensing and Communication](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16556v1)
*Trong Duy Tran,Maxime Ferreira Da Costa,Salah Eddine Elayoubi,Nguyen Linh Trung*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文研究了协作式集成感知与通信（ISAC）场景中的资源分配问题，提出了一种可扩展的网络感知框架，通过优化调度政策来平衡感知服务性能与增强移动宽带（eMBB）服务性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 智能设备的分布式协作感知和通信需求日益增长，如何在资源有限的情况下优化多设备协作感知性能，同时保障eMBB服务质量，成为一个关键问题。

Method: 作者采用了判别增益作为分类目标的代理指标，并构建了跨层优化问题，分为独立调度和联合调度两种策略。联合调度策略通过简化的增益模型降低了复杂度。两种策略均通过逐次凸逼近和参数化凸优化求解。

Result: 实验表明，这两种策略在合成和真实雷达数据集上均优于基线策略。联合调度策略在强相关性和严格通信约束下性能更优。

Conclusion: 本文提出的框架和策略能有效平衡感知与通信性能，联合调度策略在特定条件下表现更佳，但需权衡计算复杂度。

Abstract: In this paper, we consider resource allocation for a collaborative integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) scenario, in which distributed smart devices can be scheduled to perform sensing and transmit their sensing features to a fusion center. The fusion center aims to perform classification tasks on the environment based on received features. A scalable networksensing framework is proposed to balance the performance of the sensing service with that of the classical enhanced Mobile Broadband (eMBB) service. We adopt a tractable theoretical metric, the discriminant gain, as a proxy for the classification goal. We formulate cross-layer optimization problems to maximize discriminant gain under constraints on energy consumption and eMBB communication quality for the independent and joint scheduling policies. The joint scheduling policy has considerably higher complexity than the independent scheduling policy, in exchange for better collaborative sensing performance. A simplified gain model is proposed to reduce the complexity and practicality of the joint scheduling policy. Both policies are obtained via successive convex approximation and parametric convex optimization. Extensive experiments are conducted to verify the goal-oriented framework and the two policies. It is demonstrated that the two policies outperform the baseline policies with both synthetic and realistic radar simulation datasets. The joint scheduling policy can exploit device correlations and thus performs better than the independent scheduling policy under strong correlations and strict communication constraints.

Abstract: Precise aerial radio environment characterization is vital for low-altitude planning. However, existing datasets and estimation methods lack the high-resolution granularity required for complex aerial spaces. Additionally, current schemes suffer from poor generalization and heavy reliance on environmental priors. To address these gaps, this paper introduces FARM, a pioneering foundation model for unified aerial radio map estimation. This model is supported by a newly curated, high-resolution dataset featuring multi-band and multi-antenna configurations specifically for low-altitude environments. FARM utilizes a masked autoencoder to extract deep latent representations of the aerial radio environment, which then guide a diffusion-based decoder to generate high-fidelity signal distributions through iterative refinement. Extensive experiments demonstrate that FARM significantly outperforms state-of-the-art benchmarks and exhibits superior generalization capabilities across unseen scenarios. Ultimately, FARM serves as a critical infrastructure for low-altitude economy by enabling autonomous aerial logistics and intelligent urban networking.

### [3] [Joint Detection and Velocity Estimation in OFDM-ISAC Cell-Free Massive MIMO Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18056v1)
*Maryam Darabi,Sergi Liesegang,Emanuele Grossi,Stefano Buzzi*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种针对基于OFDM的无蜂窝大规模MIMO网络的用户感知-通信一体化（ISAC）系统的多普勒感知框架，通过3D双基地多普勒几何模型和广义似然比测试（GLRT）检测器，结合用户目标中心化的接入点关联方法，优化了目标速度向量估计和搜索策略。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在无蜂窝大规模MIMO网络中，现有的多普勒感知方法未能充分考虑3D双基地几何结构对检测性能的影响，尤其是在高移动性场景下可能导致的显著信噪比下降问题。本文旨在填补这一空白，提升系统的感知能力。

Method: 1. 提出了一个结合3D双基地多普勒几何模型的广义似然比测试（GLRT）检测器；2. 采用用户目标中心化的接入点关联方法以提升可扩展性；3. 开发并评估了粗网格搜索、梯度优化和粒子群优化（PSO）等速度向量估计策略；4. 推导了多普勒感知GLRT统计量和接收感知信噪比（SNR）表达式。

Result: 模拟结果表明：1. 采用粒子群优化的检测器在精度与复杂度权衡中表现最优；2. 高移动性场景下多普勒失配会导致显著的信噪比下降；3. 增加OFDM子载波数量可提升频域分集增益，进一步改善感知信噪比。

Conclusion: 本文通过引入多普勒感知框架和优化搜索策略，显著提升了无蜂窝大规模MIMO网络在感知-通信一体化中的性能，特别是在高移动性场景下的可靠性。未来可进一步探索多普勒补偿和动态网络配置的优化。

Abstract: This paper develops a Doppler-aware sensing framework for cell-free massive MIMO (CF-mMIMO) networks operating under OFDM-based integrated sensing and communication (ISAC). The framework explicitly incorporates the 3D-bistatic Doppler geometry across distributed access points (APs) into a generalized likelihood ratio test (GLRT) detector. To address the scalability, a user-target-centric AP association approach is utilized. The 3D tangential components of the target's velocity vector are estimated, and several search and optimization strategies, including coarse grid search, gradient-based refinement, and particle swarm optimization (PSO), are developed and evaluated. The Doppler-aware GLRT statistic and receive sensing signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) are derived. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed PSO-aided detector achieves the most favorable accuracy-complexity trade-off, while Doppler mismatch can cause substantial sensing-SNR degradation in high-mobility scenarios. Additionally, leveraging more OFDM subcarriers enhances frequency-domain diversity and yields further sensing-SNR gains.
