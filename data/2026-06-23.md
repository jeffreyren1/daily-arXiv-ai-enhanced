### [1] [Resolving Multi-Target Association in OFDM-based ISAC via Vision-aided Multi-Modal Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.22195v1)
*Meng Hua,Chenghong Bian,Deniz Gunduz*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种基于视觉辅助的OFDM-ISAC框架，通过融合无线和视觉模态，解决多目标场景中延迟-多普勒地图（DDM）的模糊性和目标分辨问题。研究结果表明，该方法显著提高了目标定位精度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在基于OFDM的集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统中，多目标场景下提取目标参数时存在两个主要问题：一是延迟-多普勒地图（DDM）无法区分哪些物理目标产生了哪些峰值；二是同一分辨率单元内的两个目标无法被分离。这些问题限制了ISAC系统的性能和应用场景。作者希望通过融合视觉信息来解决这些局限。

Method: 作者提出了一种视觉辅助的OFDM-ISAC框架。具体包括以下步骤：(1) 发射端使用深度联合源信道编码（DeepJSCC）将车载街景图像编码并通过OFDM波形传输；(2) 接收端重构图像，并使用微调的YOLOv5检测器提取目标特征（边界框坐标和类别标签）；(3) 将视觉模态信息与DDM和收发器几何信息通过学习的多模态网络进行融合；(4) 为稳定训练高维延迟和多普勒分类器，引入基于Kullback-Leibler损失的三角软标签。

Result: 在Blender渲染的车辆测试平台上，所提框架实现了16厘米的定位均方根误差（RMSE）和10.8纳秒的延迟RMSE。对比实验表明，移除视觉模态会导致定位性能下降60倍。

Conclusion: 该论文证明了视觉模态能够克服单模态ISAC在数据关联和分辨率方面的限制。通过融合无线和视觉信息，显著提高了目标定位的精度，为未来ISAC系统的发展提供了新的思路。

Abstract: Orthogonal frequency division multiplexing (OFDM)-based integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems commonly extract target parameters by peak-searching a delay-Doppler map (DDM) constructed from reflected pilots. In multi-target scenarios, this results in ambiguity: the DDM does not reveal which physical target produced which peak, and two targets within the same delay-Doppler resolution cell cannot be separated. We propose a vision-assisted OFDM-ISAC framework that resolves both limitations by fusing wireless and visual modalities. The transmitter encodes an onboard street-view image with deep joint source-channel coding (DeepJSCC) and transmits it over the same OFDM waveform used for sensing; the receiver reconstructs the image, runs a fine-tuned YOLOv5 detector and fuses the resulting per-target features (bounding-box coordinates and class labels) with the DDM and transmitter-receiver geometry through a learned multi-modal network. To stabilize training of the high dimensional delay and Doppler classifiers, we introduce a Kullback Leibler loss against triangular soft labels centered on the ground-truth bin. On a Blender-rendered vehicular testbed, the proposed framework achieves a 16 cm localization root mean square error (RMSE) and a 10.8 ns delay RMSE. An ablation study confirms that removing the visual modality causes a 60x degradation in localization. These results highlight the potential of vision to overcome the data-association and resolution limits of single-modality ISAC.

### [2] [Geometry-Controlled Exceptional Points in Helicoidal Orbital-Angular-Momentum Photonics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.22240v1)
*Edilberto O. Silva*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种几何控制的路径，用于在轨道角动量光子学中实现异常点（EPs），并通过螺旋扭曲背景和开放式系统设计实现EPs的生成和控制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 异常点（EPs）是非厄米特光谱奇异点，其本征值和本征向量会发生合并。作者的目标是通过几何控制的路径在轨道角动量（OAM）光子学中实现EPs，并研究其物理特性和可观测现象。

Method: 论文在一个有效的无扭螺旋背景下，利用几何扭曲提供相反OAM模式之间的手性分裂，同时通过开放性、增益、损耗、泄漏或通道耦合引入非厄米性。作者通过几何失谐补偿剩余模式分裂，并满足非厄米平衡条件，实现了EPs的控制。此外，还展示了OAM斜率在量子问题和抛物面螺旋波导中的计算，并将OAM双峰嵌入多模式空间模型中，证明EPs可以在旁观模式存在的情况下保持。

Result: 作者观察到本征值合并、相位刚性崩塌、线性EP追踪和几何环绕下的分支交换等现象，并通过OAM分辨的输入-输出响应将其映射到光谱级可观测量上，包括光谱图和三维黎曼曲面可视化。同时，在两种不同的光子学平台上进行模拟，验证了所预测的异常点及其几何线性追踪。

Conclusion: 该研究提供了一种通过几何控制实现EPs的新方法，并展示了其在光子学平台上的可行性和可观测性，为非厄米系统研究提供了新的理论和实验工具。

Abstract: Exceptional points (EPs) are non-Hermitian spectral singularities where eigenvalues and eigenvectors coalesce. We propose a geometry-controlled route to EPs in orbital-angular-momentum (OAM) photonics. In an effective torsion-free helicoidal background, the twist supplies a signed, real chiral splitting between opposite-OAM modes, while openness, gain, loss, leakage, or channel coupling, supplies the non-Hermiticity. The EP is reached when the geometric detuning compensates for a residual mode splitting and the linewidth contrast meets the non-Hermitian balance condition; the twist rate is thus the control parameter, not the source of non-Hermiticity. We obtain the OAM slope both for a confined quantum problem and, in the paraxial regime, for a helically twisted guide, and embed the OAM doublet in a multimode mode-space model to show that the EP survives spectator modes. Eigenvalue coalescence, phase-rigidity collapse, linear EP tracking with residual detuning, and branch exchange under geometric encircling provide falsifiable signatures. An OAM-resolved input-output response maps these onto spectroscopy-level observables, including spectral maps and three-dimensional Riemann-surface visualizations. A device-level paraxial simulation of two distinct photonic platforms, one reciprocal and one asymmetric-coupling Wiersig-type platform, reproduces the predicted exceptional point and its linear geometric tracking.

### [3] [Formalizing Task-Space Complexity for Zero-Shot Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20967v1)
*Jung-Hoon Cho,Heling Zhang,Siqi Du,Roy Dong,Cathy Wu*

Main category: eess.SY

TL;DR: 这篇论文研究了在上下文动态系统中的零样本泛化问题，提出了一种基于性能的方向性任务不相似性度量——有符号分歧（signed divergence），用于证明源策略类在目标上下文中的泛化能力，并提供了任务空间复杂度的具体定义和实例相关的体积边界。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的策略设计往往需要在多样化的条件下运行，单一策略通常保守，而完全自适应的方案又可能过于复杂。作者旨在解决如何通过简单的策略实现泛化控制，并为其提供性能基础的任务相似性度量和实际证明。

Method: 作者引入了有符号分歧（signed divergence）的概念，作为从源上下文到目标上下文的泛化差距的上界。通过局部平滑性假设，提出了ε容忍集的概念，并探讨了任务空间复杂度的计算。在有限源策略设置下，源选择问题被转化为集合覆盖问题，并通过贪心策略实现近似最优选择。实验部分使用了Mass-Spring-Damper系统和非线性CartPole系统进行验证。

Result: 实验结果表明，贪心选择策略能够以比均匀或随机选择策略更少的策略实现相同的ε覆盖。有符号分歧和ε容忍集不仅提供了性能基础的任务相似性度量，还为构建泛化控制提供了实用的证明。

Conclusion: 论文通过引入有符号分歧和任务空间复杂度的度量，为简单策略的泛化能力提供了理论支持和实用证明，同时展示了贪心选择策略在减少策略数量方面的有效性。这一方法为在多样化条件下实现高效控制提供了新的思路。

Abstract: Policies must operate across diverse conditions, yet a single policy is often conservative while fully adaptive schemes can be complex. We study zero-shot generalization in contextual dynamical systems and introduce a performance-centric, directional task dissimilarity--the signed divergence--that upper bounds the generalization gap from a source context to a target context. The signed divergence induces $\varepsilon$-tolerance sets that certify when a source policy class generalizes, and it yields a concrete notion of task-space complexity: the minimum number of source contexts needed so that every target context incurs at most $\varepsilon$ generalization gap. Under a mild local smoothness assumption on performance, the induced tolerance sets admit certified inner/outer balls and instance-dependent volume bounds on task-space complexity. In the finite-oracle setting, source selection reduces to set cover; a greedy strategy inherits the standard $H(n)$ approximation guarantee. Using a Mass-Spring-Damper system with linear-quadratic regulator (LQR) controllers and a nonlinear CartPole system with deep reinforcement learning controllers, we show that greedy selection achieves the same $\varepsilon$-coverage with fewer policies than uniform or random baselines. Our approach delivers a performance-based task similarity measure and practical certificates for building generalizable control with simple policies.

### [4] [Adaptive 5G Resource Allocation for Multistatic ISAC-Based UAV Detection and Tracking](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.21677v1)
*Cole Dickerson,Wahab Khawaja,Ismail Guvenc*

Main category: eess.SY

TL;DR: 本文研究了在5G无线网络中使用自适应多静态ISAC（集成感知与通信）技术来进行无人机（UAV）的检测与跟踪，并提出了一种共享资源框架，以平衡感知和通信需求。通过使用Zadoff-Chu波形和改进的检测模型，结合软件定义传感器节点（SDS）利用外部机会信号（SoO），系统在动态负载下保持高吞吐量的同时提升了感知性能。结果显示自适应策略优于固定策略，多静态几何结构也优于单静态结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 无人机（UAV）的普及带来了安全隐患，特别是在关键基础设施和受限空域附近。现有的集成感知与通信（ISAC）技术虽然可以利用无线网络进行无人机监控，但面临感知与通信资源竞争以及低雷达截面（RCS）无人机的高机动性跟踪问题。本文旨在解决这些挑战，提出一种高效的解决方案。

Method: 1. 开发了一种共享资源框架，量化了感知波形长度、传输速率和波束分配对5G新空口（NR）系统通信吞吐量的影响。2. 使用Zadoff-Chu（ZC）感知波形分析检测性能，并通过M-of-N检测模型评估跟踪连续性。3. 在资源受限时，利用软件定义传感器节点（SDS）和外部机会信号（SoO）提供被动感知支持。4. 通过Cramer-Rao下界（CRLB）分析多静态几何结构的定位精度和空间覆盖。

Result: 自适应感知策略在动态负载下优于固定策略，能够保持高通信吞吐量同时有效感知。在拥塞情况下，SDS辅助显著减少了跟踪中断。多静态几何结构比单静态结构在定位精度和空间覆盖上表现更优。

Conclusion: 本文提出了一种协调的自适应感知和分布式多静态支持框架，能够有效提升无人机监控的鲁棒性，为未来无线网络中的无人机监控提供了一种实用路径。

Abstract: Unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) enable numerous commercial and public-safety applications, yet they also create security risks near critical infrastructure, transportation hubs, and restricted airspace. While integrated sensing and communications (ISAC) can leverage existing wireless networks for UAV surveillance, practical deployment must address competition between sensing and communication demands, as well as the challenges associated with tracking highly maneuverable UAVs with low radar cross section (RCS). This paper investigates adaptive multistatic ISAC for load-aware UAV detection and tracking in 5G wireless networks. A shared-resource framework is developed to quantify how sensing waveform length, sensing transmission rate, and beam allocation affect communication throughput in a 5G new radio (NR) system. Detection performance is analyzed using Zadoff-Chu (ZC) sensing waveforms, while tracking continuity is evaluated through an M-of-N detection model. To improve robustness under congestion, software-defined sensor (SDS) nodes exploit external signals of opportunity (SoO) to provide supplemental passive sensing opportunities when network resources become limited. Results show that adaptive sensing policies outperform fixed sensing reservations by preserving throughput under dynamic load while maintaining useful sensing capability. Under heavy congestion, SDS assistance substantially reduces tracking outage in the simulated scenarios. Cramer-Rao lower bound (CRLB) analysis demonstrates that multistatic sensing geometries improve localization accuracy and provide more uniform spatial coverage than monostatic sensing alone. These results highlight coordinated adaptive sensing and distributed multistatic support as a practical path toward resilient UAV surveillance in future wireless networks.

### [5] [Anticipating the Optimism Gap: Predicting Distribution-Shift Degradation of RF-Impairment Detectors from In-Distribution Statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.22054v1)
*Chakshu Baweja*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文探讨了GNSS射频干扰检测器在不同分布条件下的性能下降问题，提出了一种通过分布内数据预测性能下降的方法，并在合成和真实数据上验证了其有效性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: GNSS射频干扰检测器通常在训练分布上表现良好，但在分布外条件下性能下降显著。由于实际标记数据稀缺，这种下降程度往往难以提前预测。本文旨在通过分析分布内数据，提前预测检测器的性能下降，以解决这一问题。

Method: 作者构建了一个开放的、参数化的合成测试平台，模拟不同程度的干扰条件。评估了13种检测器（包括物理基线、逻辑回归、多层感知机等）在四种干扰类别上的表现。通过分析分布内性能（AUC）与分布外性能的差距（乐观差距），构建了一个基于岭回归的预测模型，用于预测未知检测器或干扰类别的性能下降。

Result: 在合成数据上，乐观差距与干扰严重程度呈单调增长关系（平均Spearman相关系数0.50），且与检测器使用的观测值数量相关。岭回归模型在预测未知检测器（R²=0.47）和未知干扰类别（R²=0.46）的性能下降时表现显著。真实数据（如Jammertest 2024和SatGrid）上的验证表明，模型虽性能稍低但仍有效（R²=0.11，Spearman rho=1.0）。

Conclusion: 研究表明，通过分布内数据可以有效预测GNSS干扰检测器在分布外条件下的性能下降，且这一机制在真实数据中仍然成立。作者还公开了测试平台、软件接收前端和相关协议，为后续研究提供了工具。

Abstract: Detectors for GNSS radio-frequency impairments (jamming, spoofing, multipath) are usually reported with a single AUC measured on the distribution they were tuned on. That number falls once conditions move, and the size of the drop is rarely known in advance because labelled field data is scarce. We ask whether this optimism can be predicted before any out-of-distribution data is seen. On an open, parameter-grounded synthetic testbed with a tunable severity shift, we evaluate thirteen detectors (five physics baselines, full-feature logistic regression and multilayer perceptrons, and single-feature learned controls) across four impairment classes. The optimism gap, the difference between in-distribution and shifted AUC, grows monotonically as the shift deepens (mean Spearman correlation 0.50). It is driven by how many observables a detector uses rather than by whether it is learned, and it varies systematically by class. Centrally, a ridge model built only from in-distribution score statistics predicts the gap for a detector it has never seen (R^2 = 0.47) and for an impairment class it has never seen (R^2 = 0.46); both are significant against a 2000-fold permutation null (p < 0.001) and survive removing the feature that is, by construction, part of the target. The headline findings are synthetic. We then run the pre-registered protocol on three open field corpora: on Jammertest 2024 the cross-detector prediction holds (R^2 = 0.11, p = 0.009), and on SatGrid, whose spoofer power sweep gives a calibrated severity axis, in-distribution AUC overstates higher-severity AUC by up to 0.22 and to the point of sign inversion, with in-distribution AUC and realised gap perfectly rank-correlated (Spearman rho = 1.0). The mechanism survives contact with real data, at smaller magnitude than in simulation. We release the testbed, a software-receiver front end, the ingest adapters and the protocol.

</details>
