### [1] [Photonic neuromorphic processing with coupled spiking silicon microrings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05918)
*Giovanni Donati,Stefano Biasi,Lorenzo Pavesi,Antonio Hurtado*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 论文提出了一种基于三个微环组成的SCISSOR结构的紧凑被动非线性光子核心，研究了其计算能力，展示了在时间复用储层计算中的高性能表现，尤其在低功耗下表现出众。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 作者试图解决的问题是如何理解和利用单个网络节点的物理计算机制，以便为神经形态光子架构的扩展提供有效的构建模块。

Method: 论文采用了一种基于SCISSOR结构（三个名义上相等的微环组成的侧耦合集成空间序列谐振器）的光子核心，通过调整注入功率和波长来访问其非线性、内部反馈以及模拟、尖峰和双稳态响应。该结构被用作时间复用储层计算中的单个非线性节点，研究了其在模拟和数字储层表示下的性能。

Result: 实验结果表明，SCISSOR在Iris数据集上实现了无错误分类，并在Sonar任务上达到了97%以上的准确率。在数字模式下，尖峰动态自然地生成了稀疏储层状态，即使在单个尖峰的情况下也能高效分类。最优工作点位于动态复杂性或输出功率发生急剧变化的边界，这些点支持高任务性能。此外，尖峰和热双稳态在低注入功率（低于4 mW）下增强了计算性能。

Conclusion: 论文的结论是光学耦合微环谐振器可以作为未来边缘计算和神经形态光子系统的有效构建模块。SCISSOR结构的非线性特性和动态复杂性在低功耗条件下展现出优异的计算能力，为未来的片上训练策略提供了新的可能性。

Abstract: Understanding the physical computing mechanisms of individual network nodes is essential for scaling neuromorphic photonic architectures. This work proposes a compact passive nonlinear photonic core based on a Side-Coupled Integrated Spaced Sequence of Resonators (SCISSOR) made of three nominally equal microrings and investigate its computing capabilities. Its nonlinearities and internal feedback enable analogue, spiking, and bistable responses that are accessed by tuning the injection power and wavelength. Implemented as a single nonlinear node in a time-multiplexed reservoir computing, the SCISSOR achieves error-free classification on the Iris dataset and accuracies above 97% on the Sonar task, using both analogue and digital reservoir representations with 150 virtual nodes. In the digital scheme, spiking dynamics naturally generate sparse reservoir states, enabling efficient classification even with a single spike. Intriguingly, optimal operating points are at the boundaries where sharp transitions in dynamical complexity and/or output power occur. In these points, the SCISSOR supports high task-performance, opening novel strategies for future on-chip training. Spiking and thermal bistabilities also participate to enhance the computational performance at low injected powers below 4 mW. These results suggest optical coupled microring resonators as effective building blocks for future edge computing and neuromorphic photonic systems.

### [2] [Topological Metamaterial for Magnetic Resonance Imaging](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05256)
*Siyong Zheng,Maopeng Wu,Zhonghai Chi,Xinxin Li,Mingze Weng,Fubei Liu,Yingyi Qi,Yi Yi,Yakui Wang,Jie Gao,Guoxiang Zhan,Zewen Chen,Shuojun Ling,Yucheng Wei,Zhuozhao Zheng,Qian Zhao,Ji Zhou*

Main category: physics.app-ph

TL;DR: 论文提出了一种利用拓扑材料增强MRI信号接收的新方法，以解决传统接收线圈因通道数量和磁场强度优化面临的成本和复杂性挑战。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: MRI是医疗领域的重要工具，但其传统接收线圈的信噪比（SNR）提升受限于通道数量和磁场强度的优化，导致高成本和复杂性。作者希望通过新材料解决这一问题。

Method: 采用了一种基于拓扑材料的解决方案，通过设计具有弱耦合堆叠的拓扑材料，形成准二维双拓扑边界态。这种材料利用低损耗信号传输和增强局部磁场的方式，提高了信号接收性能。

Result: 初步测试表明，这种拓扑材料设计的线圈在性能和可用性上优于传统商业线圈，显示出显著潜力。

Conclusion: 论文提出的拓扑材料方法为MRI线圈设计带来了变革性范式，有望解决传统技术的高成本和复杂性挑战。

Abstract: Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is crucial in global healthcare, but the traditional receive coils, as a core component of MRI, SNR enhancement is limited due to the optimization of channel number and magnetic field strength faces high cost and complexity challenges. Here, we demonstrate the use of a topological material to enhance MRI signal reception. Designed with a stack of weak couplings, this material forms quasi-two-dimensional dual topological boundary states. High properties are achieved through low-loss signal transmission via these topological states, as well as only enhanced local magnetic fields and increased number of channels. Initial tests demonstrate superior performance and accessibility compared to commercial coils, suggesting significant potential. This concept introduces a transformative paradigm for all MRI coil designs.

### [3] [Self-Portrait of the Focusing Process in Speckle: I. Spatio-Temporal Imaging of Wave Packets in Complex Media](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05907)
*Elsa Giraudat,Flavien Bureau,William Lambert,Mathias Fink,Alexandre Aubry*

Main category: physics.app-ph

TL;DR: 本文是系列三篇文章中的第一篇，探讨如何利用散斑（speckle）进行成像。通过将随机分布的散射体作为虚拟麦克风，结合矩阵成像技术，监测波包在介质中的时空传播。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 旨在解决波聚焦和成像过程中因强畸变层导致的散焦和混响问题，提升成像质量。

Method: 采用矩阵成像技术，通过解耦发射和接收焦点的位置，扫描波场以生成虚拟换能器，实现对波包时空演化的成像。

Result: 在超声实验中验证了方法的可行性，展示了波包传播的时空演化及其在强畸变层下的成像缺陷。

Conclusion: 该方法为后续补偿波聚焦和成像中的畸变现象提供了基础，并可推广至其他类型的波成像领域。

Abstract: This is the first article in a series of three dealing with the exploitation of speckle for imaging purposes. Speckle is the complex interference wave-field produced by a random distribution of un-resolved scatterers. In this paper, we show how these scatterers can be used as virtual microphones to monitor the spatio-temporal propagation of a wave-packet inside the medium. To do so, the concept of matrix imaging is particularly useful. It consists in decoupling the location of the transmitted and received focal spots in a standard beamforming process. By scanning the wave-field with the output focal spot that then acts as a virtual transducer, one can image the spatio-temporal evolution of the wave-packet inside the medium. This unique observable will allow us to highlight the imperfections of the focusing process, in particular the defocus and reverberations induced by a strong aberrating layer. As a proof-of-concept, we will consider ultrasound experiments on tissue-mimicking phantoms. In the next two papers, we will show how this observable can be leveraged to compensate for these phenomena that hamper wave focusing and imaging in all fields of wave physics. Our method is indeed broadly applicable to different types of waves beyond ultrasound for which multi-element technology allows a reflection matrix to be measured.
