### [1] [Comprehensive Review of Doppler Shift Localization Methods: Advances, Limitations, and Research Opportunities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14413v1)
*Rafal Szczepanik*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文回顾了基于多普勒效应的非合作发射器定位技术，总结了十多年来在无线电、声学和卫星领域的研究成果，提出了一个分类框架，并比较了不同技术在实际场景中的性能。文章还探讨了环境特定的部署案例，并提出了未来研究方向。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在GNSS不可用或性能下降的环境中，如何可靠地对非合作发射器进行定位是实现频谱管理、应急响应、自主移动以及5G/6G系统中的集成感知与通信服务（ISAC）的关键。多普勒技术因其被动性和高分辨率特性，成为解决这一问题的理想方案。

Method: 文章提出了一种统一的分类法，将定位技术分为五个类别，并详细介绍了其演化过程、测量模型和估计方法。同时，比较了代数、贝叶斯、凸优化和神经网络等推断框架在振荡器漂移、多径效应和异步时钟等实际场景中的表现。

Result: 研究结果表明，在某些条件下，基于导数增强的多普勒度量能够以较低的硬件成本逼近克拉美罗下界。在不同环境中（如城市峡谷、水下、无人机集群等），原型系统的定位精度可达米级。

Conclusion: 文章总结了用于移动和战术操作的设计建议，并指出了未来在频率参考完整性、多径感知建模、边缘计算和轨迹感知等领域的研究挑战。

Abstract: Reliable geolocation of non-cooperative emitters in environments where Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS) are unavailable or degraded is a key enabler for spectrum regulation, emergency response, autonomous mobility, and Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) services in 5G/6G systems. Doppler-based techniques - from single-receiver Signal Doppler Frequency (SDF) fixes through multi-node Frequency Difference of Arrival (FDOA) and Direct Position Determination (DPD) to derivative-enhanced and learning-assisted hybrids - exploit radial-velocity-induced frequency shifts as a passive, high-resolution localization cue accessible with commodity software-defined radios, millimeter-wave access points, or acoustic sensors. This review consolidates over a decade of research across radio, acoustic, and satellite domains. It introduces a unifying taxonomy that divides the field into five technique families, outlining their evolution, measurement models, and estimator archetypes. It then compares algebraic, Bayesian, convex, and neural inference frameworks under realistic impairments such as oscillator drift, multipath, and asynchronous clocks, highlighting conditions where derivative Doppler metrics tighten the Cramer-Rao bound with minimal hardware cost. Environment-specific deployments are examined, from urban canyons and GNSS-denied tunnels to underwater, radar, UAV-swarm, and multi-orbit satellite scenarios, with prototype accuracies reaching meter scale using low-size, weight, and power payloads. Finally, the survey distils design recommendations for mobile and tactical operations and identifies open research challenges in frequency-reference integrity, multipath-aware modelling, edge-constrained computation, and trajectory-aware sensing.

### [2] [ISAC with Backscattering RFID Tags: Beamforming and Codebook Design](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14689v1)
*Hao Luo,Umut Demirhan,Ahmed Alkhateeb*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 该论文研究了一种基于反向散射RFID标签的集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统，旨在设计联合感知与通信波束成形的码本，以提高标签的探测范围并最小化发射功率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在现有的ISAC系统中，如何有效地协调感知与通信功能之间的相互干扰是一个关键问题。特别是当系统中引入反向散射RFID标签时，需要设计一种高效的波束成形方案，以同时满足通信用户的服务需求和标签的感知需求。

Method: 论文首先研究了单标签场景下的波束成形设计，提出了两种方法：（i）零干扰法结合优化的感知/通信功率分配，推导了在主导灵敏度条件下的闭式解；（ii）通过最小化发射功率实现联合感知与通信的波束成形设计。在多个标签的场景下，提出了一种基于扇区的联合波束成形码本方案，并使用半定松弛和广义Benders分解解决优化问题。

Result: 仿真结果表明，所提出的联合波束成形设计能够有效减少感知与通信功能之间的干扰，显著提高标签的探测范围并降低发射功率。基于扇区的码本设计在标签探测成功率方面表现出色。

Conclusion: 论文提出的联合波束成形设计和扇区码本方法为ISAC-反向散射系统提供了一种高效且实用的解决方案，能够同时优化感知与通信性能，具有较大的实际应用潜力。

Abstract: This paper explores an integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) system with backscattering RFID tags. In this setup, an access point employs communication beams to serve communication users while leveraging a sensing beam to interrogate RFID tags. Under the total transmit power constraint of the system, our objective is to design a joint sensing and communication beamforming codebook by considering the tag interrogation and communication requirements. To lay a foundation for the codebook design problem, we first study the beamforming design problem in a single-tag scenario and investigate two approaches: (i) a zero-forcing approach with optimized sensing/communication power allocation, for which a closed-form solution is derived under a dominant sensitivity condition, and (ii) a joint sensing and communication beamforming design obtained by transmit power minimization. Then, we investigate the codebook design problem in a multi-tag scenario. To resolve this, we propose a sector-based joint sensing and communication beamforming codebook that scans the region of interest. For each sector, semidefinite relaxation and generalized Benders decomposition are employed to handle the resulting optimization. The simulation results show that the proposed joint beamforming designs can effectively mitigate the mutual interference between sensing and communication functionalities, thus enhancing the interrogation range of the tags with minimized transmit power. Also, the efficacy of the proposed sector-based codebook design has been demonstrated in terms of interrogation success rate, offering a promising approach for the ISAC-backscattering systems.

### [3] [Optical Modulation Due to Energy Exchange Between Photonic and Exciton Modes in the Intermediate Coupling Regime](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15066v1)
*Evripidis Michail,Sander A. Mann,Kamyar Rashidi,Christoph Lambert,Vinod M. Menon,Andrea Alu,Matthew Y. Sfeir*

Main category: cond-mat.mtrl-sci

TL;DR: 本文针对有机光电设备在近红外（NIR）区域吸收低、响应慢及非线性弱的局限性，提出了一种基于中间激子-光子耦合的方案，实现了对光学响应的动态控制，为宽带光学信号调制提供了新途径。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 有机光电设备在近红外区域的应用受限于低吸收、慢响应和弱非线性，本文旨在通过激子-光子耦合的中间态设计，提升其性能以满足下一代光电子学对快速开关和高带宽的需求。

Method: 作者使用了一种基于近红外吸收方酸染料的激子-光子耦合结构，并通过能量-动量分辨的泵浦-探测光谱技术，结合时间耦合模式理论，分析了系统光学响应的变化规律。

Result: 实验结果表明，在激子与光子模式接近共振时，系统会出现一种能量交换过程，从而实现对光学响应的动态控制，为宽带光学信号调制提供了可能。

Conclusion: 通过中间激子-光子耦合的设计，本文成功解决了有机光电设备在近红外区域的性能限制，为下一代光电子器件的开发提供了新思路。

Abstract: Actively tunable photonic devices are vital for next-generation optoelectronics requiring rapid switching and high bandwidth. Although organic optoelectronic devices have found wide application, their use as optical modulators has been limited by low absorption in the critical near-infrared (NIR) region, slow response time, and weak nonlinearities. To address these limitations, we developed a scheme based on intermediate exciton-photon coupling in a NIR absorbing squaraine-dye based photonic structure. Using energy-momentum resolved pump-probe spectroscopy, we show that the sign and magnitude of the optical response of our system depends strongly on the energy detuning between the excitonic and photonic modes. These data are analyzed using temporal coupled-mode theory to show that near resonance, a distinct energy exchange process emerges in the cross-over regime between strong and weak light-matter coupling. This effect enables dynamical control over the photoinduced response, providing a pathway for broadband optical signal modulation extending into the NIR spectral region.

### [4] [Towards Trustworthy 6G Network Digital Twins: A Framework for Validating Counterfactual What-If Analysis in Edge Computing Resources](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14787v1)
*Julian Jimenez Agudelo,Paola Soto,Ayat Zaki-Hindi,Jean-Sébastien Sottet,Sébastien Faye,Nina Slamnik-Kriještorac,Johann Marquez-Barja,Miguel Camelo Botero*

Main category: cs.NI

TL;DR: 这篇论文提出了一个数据驱动的网络数字孪生（NDT）框架，通过扩展6G-TWIN，解决了云边基础设施中从遥测到验证的碎片化工作流问题。其贡献包括可扩展的云边遥测收集、感知网络扩展行为的特征工程，以及基于签名一致性和方向敏感性的验证方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 网络数字孪生（NDT）能够为6G云边基础设施提供安全的假设分析，但其采用通常受到从遥测到验证的碎片化工作流的限制。作者旨在通过一个数据驱动的NDT框架来解决这一问题，以支持高效、可靠的资源扩展。

Method: 论文提出了一个可扩展的框架，包括以下方法：（1）可扩展的云边遥测收集；（2）感知网络扩展行为的特征工程；（3）基于签名一致性和方向敏感性的验证方法。这些方法通过在Kubernetes管理的集群上进行评估来实现性能预测。

Result: 评估结果显示，深度神经网络（DNN）和XGBoost模型在回归任务中均实现了高精度（R2>0.99），而XGBoost模型在方向可靠性上表现更优（Sa>0.90），使得NDT在分布外场景中成为可信的资源扩展工具。

Conclusion: 论文提出的数据驱动NDT框架通过整合遥测数据和验证方法，显著提升了6G云边基础设施中资源扩展的可靠性和效率。XGBoost在方向敏感性任务上的优异表现表明其更适合于实际部署。

Abstract: Network Digital Twins (NDTs) enable safe what-if analysis for 6G cloud-edge infrastructures, but adoption is often limited by fragmented workflows from telemetry to validation. We present a data-driven NDT framework that extends 6G-TWIN with a scalable pipeline for cloud-edge telemetry aggregation and semantic alignment into unified data models. Our contributions include: (i) scalable cloud-edge telemetry collection, (ii) regime-aware feature engineering capturing the network's scaling behavior, and (iii) a validation methodology based on Sign Agreement and Directional Sensitivity. Evaluated on a Kubernetes-managed cluster, the framework extrapolates performance to unseen high-load regimes. Results show both Deep Neural Network (DNN) and XGBoost achieve high regression accuracy (R2 > 0.99), while the XGBoost model delivers superior directional reliability (Sa > 0.90), making the NDT a trustworthy tool for proactive resource scaling in out-of-distribution scenarios.
