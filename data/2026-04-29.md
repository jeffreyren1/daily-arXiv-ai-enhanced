### [1] [Joint Hybrid Beamforming and Trajectory Design for Multi-UAV-Enabled Cell-Free Multi-Static ISAC](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24600v2)
*Chen Chaoran,Zhang Yuhao,Pan Zhiwen,Liu Nan*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文研究了由多无人机支持的蜂窝自由多静态集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统中的联合混合数字-模拟波束成形和轨迹设计问题，通过优化波束成形和无人机轨迹来最大化加权和速率（WSR），同时考虑了实际约束条件。

Motivation: 研究动机在于解决在无人机支持的ISAC系统中，如何通过联合优化混合波束成形和无人机轨迹来提升通信和感知性能的问题，同时满足实际系统的约束条件。

Method: 采用了惩罚双分解（PDD）方法，将复杂的原始问题转化为可求解形式，并以联合优化混合波束成形和无人机轨迹为核心。所提方法考虑了功率预算、感知信噪比要求、无人机运动学约束以及连续和离散相移器等多个实际约束。

Result: 仿真结果表明，该设计方案在性能上接近全数字（FD）方案，并明显优于其他方案。同时，利用无人机移动性和多静态合作为系统提供了关键的额外自由度，避免了在有限发射功率或严格感知要求下的WSR性能下降。

Conclusion: 研究得出结论，所提出的联合设计方法在无人机支持的ISAC系统中有效提升了性能和灵活性，为未来实际应用提供了可行方案。

Abstract: This paper investigates a joint hybrid digital-analog beamforming and trajectory design for a cell-free multi-static integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) system supported by multiple unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). Specifically, these UAVs cooperatively serve ground users and perform multi-static sensing to detect the target. We formulate a weighted sum-rate (WSR) maximization problem by jointly optimizing the hybrid beamformers and the UAV trajectories. This joint design explicitly accounts for practical constraints, including transmit power budgets, sensing signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) requirements, UAV kinematic constraints, and both continuous and discrete phase shifters. In particular, we reformulate the original complex problem into a solvable form that can be addressed using the penalty dual decomposition (PDD) method. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed design achieves performance close to that of the fully digital (FD) scheme and significantly outperforms other schemes. Furthermore, leveraging UAV mobility and multi-static cooperation provides crucial spatial degrees of freedom, effectively avoiding WSR degradation under limited transmit power or strict sensing requirements.

### [2] [Multi-Static ISAC Assisted by Double-Side Fluid Antenna System](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25234v1)
*Qinyuan Zheng,Pengcheng Zhu*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于双端流体天线系统（DS-FAS）的多静态集成感知与通信（ISAC架构），通过联合优化天线位置和发射波束成形，以最大化目标检测概率并满足通信服务质量要求。采用基于惩罚的机制和交替优化算法解决了系统非凸性问题，并在噪声和干扰场景中验证了其优越性能。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 6G网络中的集成感知与通信（ISAC）是一个关键应用场景，但现有的系统设计难以同时满足异构连接需求和感知与通信性能的联合优化。因此，本文通过结合多静态ISAC架构和双端流体天线系统（DS-FAS），充分利用空间自由度，解决这一挑战。

Method: 本文提出了一种联合优化框架，通过优化双端流体天线位置和发射波束成形来最大化目标检测概率并满足通信服务质量要求。具体采用基于惩罚的机制解决初始可行点问题，并通过交替优化算法分解问题：利用半定松弛技术优化发射波束成形，使用主最小化方法确定发射天线位置，最后通过梯度上升法高效解决接收天线位置问题。

Result: 数值仿真结果表明，在噪声受限和干扰受限场景下，所提出的DS-FAS辅助多静态ISAC系统均表现出优越性能。优化后的系统不仅提升了目标检测概率和通信质量，还降低了复杂度。

Conclusion: 本文通过多静态ISAC架构和DS-FAS的联合优化，显著提升了感知和通信的联合性能。提出的算法在非凸问题处理上具有鲁棒性和高效性，为6G网络中ISAC的实际部署提供了关键见解和技术支持。

Abstract: As a pivotal usage scenario for 6G networks, integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) has emerged as a focal point of both academic and industrial research. To accommodate the heterogeneous connectivity requirements of future networks while jointly enhancing both the sensing and communication performance, this paper integrates the multi-static ISAC architecture with double-side fluid antenna system (DS-FAS) to fully exploit the available spatial degrees-of-freedom. Specifically, we establish a joint optimization framework for FA positions and transmit beamforming to maximize the target detection probability while satisfying the communication quality-of-service requirements. Recognizing the intricate coupling between the double-side FA positions and transmit beamforming, instead of trying to obtain an initial feasible point, we resort to the penalty-based mechanism to ensure the robustness against initial feasibility without introducing additional non-convexity. An alternating optimization-based algorithm is proposed to solve the decoupled subproblems. Specifically, the transmit beamforming is globally optimized via the semidefinite relaxation technique, while the transmit FA positions are determined using the majorization-minimization method. Finally, leveraging the analyzed FA mechanism, the feasibility subproblem for receive FA positions is transformed into a signal-to-interference-plus-noise ratio maximization one, solved efficiently via a gradient ascent-based approach, which yields superior performance over the feasibility-based benchmark with reduced complexity. Numerical results demonstrate the superiority of the considered DS-FAS-assisted multi-static ISAC systems in both noise-limited and interference-limited scenarios, while key insights for practical deployment are further extracted from the simulation analysis.
