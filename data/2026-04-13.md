### [1] [Balancing Functionality and GDPR-Driven Privacy in ISAC Trajectory Sharing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08743v1)
*Zexin Fang,Bin Han,Zhuojun Tian,Hans D. Schotten*

Main category: cs.NI

TL;DR: 论文提出了一个基于费舍尔信息密度约束的轨迹共享框架，以解决联合感知和通信（ISAC）中因GDPR数据最小化原则引发的隐私问题。该方法通过限制估计不确定性的局部下限，提供了硬性、可量化的隐私保证。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在联合感知与通信（ISAC）中，轨迹共享能够提升波束成形、资源分配和协作感知的性能，但也引发了隐私问题，尤其是需要满足GDPR的数据最小化原则。现有的固定噪声方法无法确保隐私泄漏比例（PLR）的全局控制，因此需要一种新的隐私保护框架。

Method: 论文提出了一种基于费舍尔信息密度（FID）的约束方法，通过限制估计不确定性的局部下限来提供隐私保证。这一方法独立于感知功率或对抗性后处理，确保任何轨迹段都无法以超出预设精度阈值的精度被重建。

Result: 在OpenTraj数据集上的仿真表明，该框架能将平均隐私泄漏比例（PLR）控制在20-25%以下，最大泄漏段的持续时间限制在2-2.5秒内，同时保持数据对下游任务（如运动预测）的可用性。

Conclusion: 提出的FID约束框架具有可解释性、模型无关性，并且与符合GDPR的ISAC系统设计兼容。它为解决轨迹共享中的隐私问题提供了一种有效且实用的解决方案。

Abstract: Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC) enables trajectory sharing that enhances beamforming, resource allocation, and cooperative perception, yet raises fundamental privacy concerns under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) data minimisation principle. This paper proposes a Fisher Information Density (FID)-constrained trajectory sharing framework that enforces a local lower bound on estimation uncertainty, providing hard, quantifiable privacy guarantees by construction. Unlike fixed-noise approaches, the proposed method bounds the Privacy Leak Ratio (PLR) regardless of sensing power or adversarial post-processing, ensuring that no trajectory segment can be reconstructed beyond a prescribed accuracy threshold. Simulations on the OpenTraj dataset demonstrate that the framework keeps the average PLR below 20-25% and the maximum leakage segment duration under 2-2.5 s, while preserving data utility for downstream tasks such as movement prediction. The resulting criterion is interpretable, model-agnostic, and compatible with GDPR-compliant ISAC system design.

### [2] [PSIRNet: Deep Learning-based Free-breathing Rapid Acquisition Late Enhancement Imaging](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08781v1)
*Arda Atalik,Hui Xue,Rhodri H. Davies,Thomas A. Treibel,Daniel K. Sodickson,Michael S. Hansen,Peter Kellman*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: PSIRNet是一种基于深度学习的自由呼吸PSIR LGE心脏MRI成像方法，能显著缩短采集时间并提升图像质量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前自由呼吸PSIR LGE心脏MRI需要多次信号平均以校正运动伪影，导致采集时间过长（8到24次）。作者旨在开发一种深度学习方法，仅通过两次心跳采集即可生成诊断质量的图像，从而大幅缩短扫描时间。

Method: 作者提出了PSIRNet，一个包含8.45亿参数的物理引导深度学习网络，用于从两次心跳的IR/PD采集数据中重建PSIR图像。网络通过端到端训练，结合表面线圈校正。训练和测试数据来自不同机构且无重叠，以确保泛化性。评估指标包括SSIM、PSNR、NRMSE，并由两位心脏专家进行定性评分。

Result: 实验结果表明，PSIRNet的单次采集重建结果在暗血LGE中优于传统的运动校正PSIR（P = .002）；在亮血和宽频LGE中，一位专家评为优越，另一位评分为等效（P < .001）。同时，推理时间显著缩短（100毫秒每切片 vs. 5秒以上）。

Conclusion: PSIRNet能够通过单次采集生成诊断质量的自由呼吸PSIR LGE图像，将采集时间缩短8至24倍，具有显著的临床应用潜力。

Abstract: Purpose: To develop and evaluate a deep learning (DL) method for free-breathing phase-sensitive inversion recovery (PSIR) late gadolinium enhancement (LGE) cardiac MRI that produces diagnostic-quality images from a single acquisition over two heartbeats, eliminating the need for 8 to 24 motion-corrected (MOCO) signal averages.   Materials and Methods: Raw data comprising 800,653 slices from 55,917 patients, acquired on 1.5T and 3T scanners across multiple sites from 2016 to 2024, were used in this retrospective study. Data were split by patient: 640,000 slices (42,822 patients) for training and the remainder for validation and testing, without overlap. The training and testing data were from different institutions. PSIRNet, a physics-guided DL network with 845 million parameters, was trained end-to-end to reconstruct PSIR images with surface coil correction from a single interleaved IR/PD acquisition over two heartbeats. Reconstruction quality was evaluated using SSIM, PSNR, and NRMSE against MOCO PSIR references. Two expert cardiologists performed an independent qualitative assessment, scoring image quality on a 5-point Likert scale across bright blood, dark blood, and wideband LGE variants. Paired superiority and equivalence (margin = 0.25 Likert points) were tested using exact Wilcoxon signed-rank tests at a significance level of 0.05 using R version 4.5.2.   Results: Both readers rated single-average PSIRNet reconstructions superior to MOCO PSIR for dark blood LGE (conservative P = .002); for bright blood and wideband, one reader rated it superior and the other confirmed equivalence (all P < .001). Inference required approximately 100 msec per slice versus more than 5 sec for MOCO PSIR.   Conclusion: PSIRNet produces diagnostic-quality free-breathing PSIR LGE images from a single acquisition, enabling 8- to 24-fold reduction in acquisition time.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 论文的动机是解决自动驾驶车辆在无信号交叉路口协作时的去中心化决策问题。现有的集中式方法在混合意图和协调器故障的情况下难以扩展，因此需要一种无需集中协调器的解决方案。

Method: 论文提出了一种基于闭环意见动态的决策模型，车辆通过双重签名网络交换意图：一是基于冲突拓扑的通信网络，二是驱动承诺的信念网络。通过连续意见状态调整速度优化器的权重，并使用闭式预测可行性门冻结车辆的决策为“GO”或“YIELD”，随后通过信念网络传播以预调邻居行为。交叉顺序由几何可行性和到达优先级决定，避免了联合优化或求解器的使用。

Result: 该方法在三种场景（完全竞争、合并和混合冲突拓扑）下验证，结果表明在所有非平凡冲突配置中实现了无碰撞协作，并且比先到先服务（FCFS）方法降低了最后一辆车的离开时间。

Conclusion: 论文的结论是，所提出的去中心化决策模型能够有效解决自动驾驶车辆在无信号交叉路口的协作问题，避免了集中式方法的局限性，并通过双重网络机制实现了高效的冲突解决和时序优化。

Abstract: In this letter, we consider the problem of decentralized decision making among connected autonomous vehicles at unsignalized intersections, where existing centralized approaches do not scale gracefully under mixed maneuver intentions and coordinator failure. We propose a closed-loop opinion-dynamic decision model for intersection coordination, where vehicles exchange intent through dual signed networks: a conflict topology based communication network and a commitment-driven belief network that enable cooperation without a centralized coordinator. Continuous opinion states modulate velocity optimizer weights prior to commitment; a closed-form predictive feasibility gate then freezes each vehicle's decision into a GO or YIELD commitment, which propagates back through the belief network to pre-condition neighbor behavior ahead of physical conflicts. Crossing order emerges from geometric feasibility and arrival priority without the use of joint optimization or a solver. The approach is validated across three scenarios spanning fully competitive, merge, and mixed conflict topologies. The results demonstrate collision-free coordination and lower last-vehicle exit times compared to first come first served (FCFS) in all conflict non-trivial configurations.

Our approach guarantees the simultaneous identification and synchronization of the unknown signed network and establishes uniform semiglobal practical asymptotic stability of the estimation errors. Numerical simulations validate our theoretical results.

### [5] [Radio Stripe-Based Distributed ISAC System with Dynamic Sensing-Communication Reconfiguration](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08982v1)
*Osmel Martínez Rosabal,Onel L. Alcaraz López*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 这篇论文研究了在即将到来的6G无线系统中，集成感知与通信（ISAC）作为一种内在服务，通过分布式系统（如无线条带）实现协同感知与通信的权衡。重点探讨了在利用下行链路信号进行目标定位时，总速率与感知精度之间的折衷。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着无线基础设施向分布式系统演进，通过空间分离节点实现协同感知成为可能。作者受此趋势启发，研究了无线条带辅助的ISAC系统，这是一种低复杂度的分布式系统实现方式，旨在解决如何在通信和感知任务之间动态配置资源的问题。

Method: 论文利用了无线条带收发器的架构同质性，允许每个单元动态配置为通信或感知模式。针对多配置测量的目标定位问题，由于测量数据量大和搜索空间连续，作者提出了将服务区域离散化并按批次解决估计问题。最终，采用融合策略估计目标位置。

Conclusion: 论文揭示了在分布式ISAC系统中，通信与感知性能之间的复杂关系，提出了动态配置和离散化策略以优化系统性能。研究结果为未来6G网络的设计提供了重要参考，尤其是在资源分配和协同感知方面。

We study the trade-off between achievable sum rate and sensing precision when downlink signals are used for target localization within the service area. By exploiting the architectural homogeneity of the radio stripes transceivers, each unit can be dynamically configured to operate in either communication or sensing mode. We formulate a targets localization problem considering the measurements of multiple sensing-communication configurations. Due to the large number of measurements and the continuity of the search space, we propose discretizing the service are and then solve the estimation problem in batches. The targets are finally estimated using a fusion strategy. Our results show that increasing the number devices and sensing APUs boosts sensing precision at the expense of degrading the sum rate. The latter remains constant for a given number of communication APUs regardless of their positions. Moreover, changing the number of antennas reveals a non-monotonic impact on sensing performance due to the trade-off between array gain and illumination uniformity.

### [6] [Periodic OFDMA: A Low-PAPR Multiple Access Scheme for Uplink Communications in 5G and Beyond](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09339v1)
*Gokce Hacioglu,Serkan Vela*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为周期性正交频分多址接入（P-OFDMA）的新型多址接入方案，旨在解决OFDMA在5G及未来网络中高峰均功率比（PAPR）和计算复杂度的问题。通过周期性分配子载波并引入两种预编码变体（P-OFDMA-DCT和P-OFDMA-DFT），P-OFDMA显著降低了PAPR和发射端处理复杂度，同时提高了频率分集性能和能效。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在5G及未来的无线网络中，正交频分多址接入（OFDMA）由于其高峰均功率比（PAPR）而降低了上行链路的能效。因此，需要一种既能降低PAPR又能简化计算复杂度的新型多址接入方案。

Method: 本文提出了周期性OFDMA（P-OFDMA），通过周期性分配子载波并引入两种预编码变体（P-OFDMA-DCT和P-OFDMA-DFT）。这些方法增强了频率分集，简化了子载波分配，并显著降低了发射端的处理复杂度。

Result: 仿真结果表明，P-OFDMA-DFT在所有方案中实现了最低的PAPR。标准P-OFDMA在低子载波用户情景下优于SC-FDMA的PAPR性能，并在高延迟扩展条件下表现出更优的误码率（BER）。此外，P-OFDMA及其变体将发射端处理复杂度降低了最多8倍，尽管接收端复杂度略有增加，但整体系统处理负载降低，提升了能效。

Conclusion: P-OFDMA为未来的无线网络提供了一种强大且能效高的上行链路解决方案，尤其适用于低复杂度上行设备，具有显著的性能优势和应用潜力。

Abstract: Multiple access techniques are vital for 5G and beyond. While Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiple Access (OFDMA) is standard, its high peak-to-average power ratio (PAPR) reduces energy efficiency in uplink transmissions. This paper presents Periodic OFDMA (P-OFDMA), a novel multiple access scheme with reduced PAPR and computational complexity. By assigning subcarriers in a periodic pattern across the entire frequency band, P-OFDMA enhances frequency diversity and simplifies allocation. We also introduce two precoded variants: P-OFDMA-DCT and P-OFDMA-DFT. Comprehensive simulations comparing P-OFDMA with OFDMA and SC-FDMA show that P-OFDMA-DFT consistently achieves the lowest PAPR. Furthermore, the standard P-OFDMA scheme outperforms SC-FDMA in PAPR for low subcarrier-per-user scenarios and achieves better bit error rate (BER) performance under high delay-spread conditions. Notably, P-OFDMA and its variants reduce transmitter-side processing by up to an eightfold factor compared to SC-FDMA, greatly benefiting low-complexity uplink devices. Although receiver complexity increases, the overall system processing load decreases, yielding improved energy efficiency. Thus, P-OFDMA offers a robust, energy-efficient uplink solution for future wireless networks.
