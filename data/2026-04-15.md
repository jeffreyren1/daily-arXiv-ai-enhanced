### [1] [Fundamental Limits of 1-bit ISAC Systems: Capacity Region and Optimal Power Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12953v1)
*Emmanuel Trinidad,Neil Irwin Bernardo*

Main category: cs.IT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了带有1位接收器量化的集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统的基本限制，分析了高斯衰落ISAC信道，探讨了通信与感知性能之间的关系，并提出了在不同场景下的功率控制策略。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着无线通信技术的发展，集成感知与通信（ISAC）系统在未来的6G网络中扮演重要角色。然而，1位量化器的使用在ISAC系统中引入了新的挑战，特别是在性能极限和系统设计方面。作者旨在解决1位量化ISAC系统的通信与感知之间的性能权衡问题，并探索如何在不同的信道状态信息（CSI）条件下优化系统性能。

Method: 论文首先分析了具有1位量化的高斯衰落ISAC信道，其中通信和感知链路分别独立。在接收器已知通信CSI的情况下，作者表征了1位ISAC信道的通信-感知容量区域，并展示了通过特定旋转对称性的恒定振幅输入分布可以同时实现通信和感知容量的最大化。此外，在发射器也已知通信CSI的情况下，作者提出了一个加权优化问题，用于在平均功率约束下平衡通信和感知速率，并导出了最优功率控制策略。

Result: 研究结果表明，在1位ISAC系统中，通信和感知性能之间不存在权衡关系。通过采用具有特定旋转对称性的恒定振幅输入分布，可以同时实现两者的性能最大化。此外，最优功率控制策略随着权重参数的演变，从通信为中心的水填充结构逐步过渡到更均匀的分配策略，尤其是在感知需求增加的情况下。

Conclusion: 本文证明了1位量化ISAC系统中通信与感知性能可以同时优化的可能性，并提出了一种灵活的功率控制策略，适用于不同的需求优先级。这项研究为未来的ISAC系统设计提供了理论基础，尤其是在低分辨率量化的限制条件下。

Abstract: This paper investigates the fundamental limits of integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems with 1-bit receiver quantization. We analyze a Gaussian fading ISAC channel with separate communication and monostatic sensing links, where both communication and sensing receivers are equipped with 1-bit quantizers. When the communication channel state information (CSI) is available at the receiver, we characterize the communication-sensing capacity region of 1-bit ISAC channel and show that no trade-off exists between communication and sensing performance. In particular, both communication and sensing capacities can be simultaneously achieved by a constant-amplitude input distribution with a specific rotational symmetry. For the scenario where communication CSI is also available at the transmitter, we formulate a weighted optimization problem that balances communication and sensing rates in 1-bit ISAC channel under an average power constraint and then derive the corresponding optimal power control policy. The results demonstrate how the optimal power control policy evolves with the weighting parameter, transitioning from a communication-centric water-filling structure to a more uniform allocation as sensing becomes increasingly prioritized.

### [2] [RIS-Aided Sensing: Experimental Validation of Radar 3D Imaging in the mmWave Band](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12466v1)
*Sergio Micó-Rosa,Alvaro Villaescusa-Tebar,Saúl Fenollosa,Carlos Villena-Jiménez,Monika Drozdowska,Narcis Cardona*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种结合FMCW雷达、1-bit RIS和VNA的自主毫米波3D成像框架，用于解决6G网络中自主3D空间映射的挑战，并在实验中验证了其有效性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 6G网络需要高效能与环境互动的硬件，RIS是实现集成传感与通信（ISAC）的关键技术。然而，实现完全自主的闭环3D空间映射仍是一个重大挑战，本文旨在解决这一问题。

Method: 通过FMCW雷达作为粗略定位器，提供实时空间先验，定义动态ROI，再通过优化的RIS相位配置文件进行SFCW测量。实验验证了系统在多种场景下的性能。

Result: 实验验证了系统在金属人体模型、校准球体及包含人类和AGV的复杂多目标环境中的准确3D体素重建能力，即使在低角分辨率下也表现良好。

Conclusion: 该研究为基于RIS的工业与安全应用提供了可行方案，展示了其在复杂环境中高效实现3D映射的潜力。

Abstract: The transition toward 6G networks demands energy-efficient hardware capable of active interaction with the environment. Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) have emerged as a key technology for Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC), enabling geometric environment recognition with minimal power consumption. However, achieving targeted 3D spatial mapping in a fully autonomous, closed-loop system remains a significant challenge. In this work, we validate experimentally an autonomous mmWave 3D imaging framework that integrates an Frequency-Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) radar with a 1-bit RIS and a Vector Network Analyzer (VNA) to perform targeted 3D reconstruction. The FMCW radar acts as a coarse localizer, providing real-time spatial priors to define dynamic Regions of Interest (ROI). These coordinates are translated into optimized RIS phase profiles to perform Stepped-Frequency Continuous-Wave (SFCW) measurements. We experimentally validate the system through three diverse scenarios, including metallic mannequins, calibration spheres, and a complex multi-target environment containing human subjects and an Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV). The results demonstrate accurate 3D voxel-based reconstruction of targets even at reduced angular resolutions, advancing the feasibility of RIS-based sensing for industrial and security applications.

### [3] [Magnetic switching of self-hybridized exciton-polaritons in CrSBr photonic crystal slabs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11750v1)
*T. D. Gorelkina,I. E. Kalantaevskii,A. N. Abramov,K. A. Gasnikova,P. A. Alekseev,X. Zeng,D. Huang,T. Jiang,I. V. Iorsh,I. Y. Chestnov,V. Kravtsov*

Main category: cond-mat.mes-hall

TL;DR: 本文研究了CrSBr薄片制成的光子晶体板中自杂化激子-极化子的行为，及其在反铁磁-铁磁自旋翻转过程中的演变，并通过磁场实现了极化子传播方向的完全切换。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: CrSBr作为一种层状范德华反铁磁体，支持强光-物质耦合和可磁调谐的激子-极化子形成，但此前未能实现对其传播方向的主动磁控制。本文旨在解决这一问题，探索磁场对极化子传播方向的调控能力。

Method: 通过角度分辨反射光谱和光致发光光谱，结合理论建模，研究了CrSBr光子晶体板中激子-极化子在反铁磁-铁磁转变过程中的能量变化，并分析了振荡强度的重新分布。实验中使用中等强度的面内磁场诱导自旋翻转，并通过小至40 mT的磁场变化实现极化子群速度符号的反转。

Result: 研究结果表明，极化子能量能够连续跟踪逐层磁化翻转过程，揭示了临界场附近从反铁磁激子到铁磁激子的振荡强度逐渐重新分布。更重要的是，仅需40 mT的磁场变化即可逆转极化子群速度的符号，从而完全切换极化子的传播方向。

Conclusion: CrSBr光子晶体板为实现磁性调控的极化子传输提供了一个新平台，为主动集成光子和极化子器件的发展开辟了新的机会。

Abstract: Layered van der Waals antiferromagnet CrSBr supports strong light--matter coupling and formation of magnetically tunable exciton-polaritons, yet active magnetic control over polariton propagation direction has remained elusive. Here, we investigate self-hybridized exciton-polaritons in photonic crystal slabs fabricated from CrSBr flakes and their evolution across the antiferromagnetic-to-ferromagnetic spin-flip transition induced by moderate in-plane magnetic fields. Using angle-resolved reflectance and photoluminescence spectroscopy supported by modeling, we show that the polariton energy continuously tracks the layer-by-layer magnetization switching, revealing a gradual redistribution of oscillator strength from antiferromagnetic to ferromagnetic excitons near the critical field. Most notably, we demonstrate that the sign of the polariton group velocity can be reversed by a small change in the external magnetic field of only 40 mT, resulting in complete switching of the polariton propagation direction. Our results establish CrSBr photonic crystal slabs as a platform for magnetically controlled polariton transport, opening opportunities for active integrated photonic and polaritonic devices.

### [4] [Thermal Characterization of Buried Interfaces in Multilayer Heterostructures via TDTR with Periodic Waveform Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12539v1)
*Mingzhen Zhang,Puqing Jiang,Ronggui Yang*

Main category: cond-mat.mtrl-sci

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种频率可调的周期性波形分析时域热反射（PWA-TDTR）技术，用于对埋入式热界面进行深度分辨测量，克服传统方法的局限性，并成功应用于多种半导体材料系统的热性能评估。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的时域热反射（TDTR）技术由于调制频率范围有限，仅能探测近表面的热传输性能，无法满足对埋入式热界面进行精确评估的需求。为解决这一问题，作者提出了一种改进的方法，以实现对材料系统内部热特性的全面分析。

Method: 作者采用了频率可调的PWA-TDTR技术，结合宽带多频探测和灵敏度引导的联合拟合，定量确定了埋入界面的热导率、层特定热导率和体积热容，且无需破坏性样品制备。该方法通过对代表性系统（如ε-Ga2O3/SiC、GaN/Si和GaN/金刚石）的测试，验证了其有效性。

Result: 研究结果表明：ε-Ga2O3/SiC界面的声子传输较弱是由于声学失配；GaN/Si中的过渡层充当了声子阻抗梯度，重新分配了热通量；尽管金刚石具有超高体热导率，GaN/金刚石界面仍是主要的热瓶颈。这些发现揭示了调制频率在深度依赖性声子传输中的关键作用。

Conclusion: 论文证明了PWA-TDTR技术作为一种多功能平台，可用于研究下一代高功率和光电材料中的非金属-非金属埋入式界面。通过频率域热响应与界面能量传输的直接关联，该方法为材料热性能的优化提供了新工具。

Abstract: Accurate evaluation of buried thermal interfaces is vital for understanding and optimizing heat dissipation in wide- and ultra-wide-bandgap (WBG/UWBG) semiconductor devices. Conventional time-domain thermoreflectance (TDTR) typically probes only near-surface transport due to its restricted modulation frequency range. Here, we employ a frequency-tunable periodic waveform analysis TDTR (PWA-TDTR) technique to perform depth-resolved thermal measurements on three representative systems: epitaxial ε-Ga2O3/SiC, GaN/Si, and mechanically bonded GaN/diamond. By combining broadband multi-frequency probing with sensitivity-guided joint fitting, we quantitively determine interfacial thermal conductance, layer-specific thermal conductivity, and volumetric heat capacity, without requiring destructive sample preparation. The results reveal that the buried Ga2O3/SiC interface exhibits weak phonon transmission due to acoustic mismatch; the transition layers in GaN/Si act as phonon-impedance gradients that redistribute heat flux; and the GaN/diamond boundary remains the dominant thermal bottleneck despite diamond's ultrahigh bulk conductivity. These findings demonstrate that the modulation frequency in PWA-TDTR functions as a tunable probe of depth-dependent phonon transport, directly linking frequency-domain thermal response to interfacial energy transmission. Overall, this work positions PWA-TDTR as a versatile platform for investigating buried nonmetal-nonmetal interfaces in next-generation high-power and optoelectronic materials.

### [5] [Signed DeGroot-Friedkin Dynamics with Interdependent Topics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12685v1)
*Yangyang Luan,Muhammad Ahsan Razaq,Xiaoqun Wu,Claudio Altafini*

Main category: eess.SY

TL;DR: 本文研究了带符号影响力网络中DeGroot-Friedkin（DF）动力学在相互依赖主题下的行为，提出了结合排斥性人际互动和跨议题自我评估的多主题带符号框架，分析了敌意和主题依赖性如何影响个体社会权力的演化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 社会网络中个体权力的动态演化是一个复杂的问题，尤其是在带符号影响力网络和相互依赖主题的背景下。本文旨在探索这些因素如何影响社会权力的形成和演化，以扩展对多主题带符号系统中社会权力动力学的理解。

Method: 作者采用了多主题带符号框架，结合了排斥性人际互动和跨议题自我评估的逻辑矩阵。通过分析这些矩阵的共同主导左特征向量，提出了将原始动力学精确简化为显式标量DF映射的条件。此外，还研究了在结构扰动下的局部鲁棒性。

Result: 研究发现，当所有主体的逻辑矩阵共享共同主导左特征向量时，社会权力的极限配置可分为多元化、混合型和顶点主导型三种类型。在所有情况下，动力学都是全局收敛的，且在前两种情况下保留了相互作用中心性诱导的顺序。研究还揭示了当共同特征向量结构缺失时的变化。

Conclusion: 本文扩展了带符号社会权力动力学的研究范围，超越了传统的非负标量设定，揭示了多主题带符号影响力系统中基于中心性的社会权力形成的鲁棒性和适用范围。研究结果为理解复杂社交网络中的权力动态提供了新的理论工具。

Abstract: This paper investigates DeGroot-Friedkin (DF) dynamics over signed influence networks with interdependent topics. We propose a multi-topic signed framework that combines repelling interpersonal interactions with cross-issue self-appraisal, examining how antagonism and topic interdependence shape the evolution of agent-level social power. When the logic matrices (for topic interdependence) of all agents share a common dominant left eigenvector, we identify structural conditions under which the original dynamics admit an exact reduction to an explicit scalar DF map. This yields a complete classification of limiting social power configurations into pluralistic, mixed, and vertex-dominant types. In all three cases, the dynamics are globally convergent, and in the first two the ordering induced by the interaction centrality is preserved. We further show local robustness under small heterogeneous perturbations of the logic matrices. We also clarify what changes when this common-eigenvector structure is lost. These results extend signed social power dynamics beyond the standard nonnegative scalar setting and shed light on the robustness and scope of centrality-based social power formation in multi-topic signed influence systems.
