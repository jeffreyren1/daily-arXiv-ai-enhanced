### [1] [ISAC for Sea-Air Networks: Predictive Beam Tracking under Sea Induced Disturbances](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26569v1)
*Rui Zhang,Fuwang Dong,Wei Wang,Zhen Du*

Main category: eess.SY

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于集成传感与通信（ISAC）的预测性波束跟踪方案，用于解决海空网络中无人机（UAV）对无人水面艇（USV）的波束失准问题。通过宽窄波束切换和时间分配优化，实现了高精度的目标跟踪和高效通信。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在海空通信网络中，由于USV的扩展目标特性和海洋扰动下的三维运动，UAV跟踪USV时容易发生波束失准。这一问题影响了通信质量和目标跟踪的稳定性，因此需要一种能同时兼顾高精度跟踪和高效通信的解决方案。

Method: 本文提出了一种宽窄波束切换方案，基于子阵列选择和时间分配优化。宽波束模式用于全覆盖和状态感知，窄波束模式利用估计状态实现高增益通信。此外，通过建立海空状态演化模型和扩展卡尔曼滤波（EKF）方法进行CR状态预测与估计，并将海杂波对感知精度的影响纳入时间分配优化问题中。

Result: 仿真结果表明，所提方案在跟踪精度上优于现有的基准方案，能够有效平衡状态感知和高速通信的需求。

Conclusion: 该方案为海空网络中波束跟踪问题提供了一种高效的解决方案，通过ISAC框架下的宽窄波束切换和时间分配优化，显著提升了系统性能。

Abstract: In sea-air communication networks composed of an uncrewed aerial vehicle (UAV) and an uncrewed surface vehicle (USV), the extended target characteristics and three degree of freedom motion of the USV under sea induced disturbances cause beam misalignment in the UAV's tracking of the USV. To address these issues, this paper proposes a predictive beam tracking scheme based on integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) for sea-air networks. We develop a wide and narrow beam switching scheme based on sub-array selection, where a time allocation factor is optimized to balance robust state sensing in the wide beam mode and high-rate communication in the narrow beam mode. Specifically, a wide beam mode provides full USV coverage and state sensing, while a narrow beam mode exploits the estimated state for high-gain communication with the communication receiver (CR) mounted on the USV. To characterize the CR motion, a sea-air state evolution model is derived by jointly considering the surge, sway, yaw, and sea induced disturbances of the USV. For the extended target USV, the measurement equation is constructed from multiple scatterer observations, with the measurement noise caused by sea clutter modeled, and an extended Kalman filter (EKF) based CR state prediction and estimation method is developed. In addition, the effect of sea clutter on sensing accuracy is incorporated into the time allocation optimization problem to adjust the time of the wide beam mode. Simulation results demonstrate that the proposed scheme achieves higher tracking accuracy than the state-of-the-art benchmark schemes.

### [2] [Parametric Generalized Adaptive Moment Features (PG-AMF) for Bearing Fault Diagnosis and Machine Health Monitoring](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26317v1)
*Rajeev Kumar*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种参数化自适应特征提取框架，用于提升滚动轴承故障诊断的准确性和适应性，解决了传统方法依赖预定义特征和深度学习方法数据需求高、可解释性差的问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 滚动轴承的精确故障诊断对工业安全和预测性维护至关重要。传统统计特征方法依赖预定义描述符，适应性受限；深度学习方法虽具强大表征能力，但数据要求高且可解释性差。因此，需要一种自适应特征提取框架以提升诊断效果。

Method: 提出的框架通过数据直接学习特征特性，而非人工指定。从振动信号中提取多组互补特征，包括能量分布特征、波形不对称特征和动态波动特征，并通过结构化融合机制建模多传感器通道的交互，增强故障表征能力。

Result: 在包含五种健康状态的基准齿轮箱轴承数据集上验证，与传统方法相比，分类性能显著提升，且交叉验证结果一致，表明具有强泛化能力。低维投影中特征可分性增强，聚类模式更清晰。

Conclusion: 该框架能够有效捕捉信号特性，提升故障诊断性能，兼具强泛化能力和工业监测系统的实用性。

Abstract: Accurate fault diagnosis of rolling element bearings in rotating machinery is considered essential for ensuring industrial safety and enabling predictive maintenance. Conventional statistical feature-based methods rely on predefined descriptors, whose diagnostic sensitivity is constrained by fixed configurations and limited adaptability across varying fault conditions. Although deep learning approaches offer strong representational capacity, their effectiveness is often restricted by high data requirements and reduced interpretability. In this work, a parametric adaptive feature extraction framework is proposed, in which feature characteristics are learned directly from data rather than being manually specified. Multiple complementary representations are extracted from vibration signals, including absolute features capturing signal energy distribution, signed moment features reflecting waveform asymmetry, and AC-coupled moment features emphasizing dynamic fluctuations, while interactions between multiple sensor channels are modeled through a structured fusion mechanism to enhance fault representation. The proposed approach is evaluated on a benchmark gearbox bearing dataset comprising five health conditions, including normal operation and multiple fault types. Improved classification performance is observed compared to conventional methods, with consistent results under cross-validation, indicating strong generalization capability. Additionally, enhanced feature separability is demonstrated through clearer clustering patterns in low-dimensional projections. The learned representations effectively capture a wide range of signal characteristics, supporting both improved diagnostic performance and practical applicability in industrial monitoring systems.
