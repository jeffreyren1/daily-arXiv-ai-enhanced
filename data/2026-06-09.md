### [1] [Complex-gauge control of anomalous Floquet corner responses in a non-Hermitian physical-synthetic photonic lattice](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07038v2)
*W. C. Ning,X. Z. Zhang*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种由物理谐振器坐标和合成频率坐标组成的非厄米Floquet光子晶格。通过两步调制协议实现了在该物理-合成平面中的手性行走，利用实合成通量控制环路干扰，虚规范场控制非互易包络。文中展示了准能量为零和π/T时的异常角对三种不同的物理层次，并通过非Bloch高阶构造预测开边界下0/π角对的存在性。虚规范场选择右本征模的积累位置，而实通量控制决定双周期光学响应是否可见的局部干扰矩阵元素。此外，复杂的规范可以调节双周期角传播器的例外点，在此点异常响应保持其双周期符号交替，但因其Jordan块而包络变为代数式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 解决在非厄米合成维度中，如何实现拓扑存在、皮肤选择定位、光学可见性以及缺陷双周期动力学的分离。这为光子学提供了一个新的研究途径。

Method: 采用非厄米Floquet光子晶格模型，结合物理谐振器坐标和合成频率坐标。通过两步调制协议实现手性行走，并利用实合成通量和虚规范场分别控制环路干扰和非互易包络。通过非Bloch高阶构造预测开边界下的拓扑存在性。

Result: 展示了异常角对的三种不同物理层次，明确了虚规范场和实通量的作用机制。发现了复杂的规范可以调节双周期角传播器的例外点，异常响应的双周期符号交替保持但包络变为代数式。

Conclusion: 研究结果为非厄米合成维度中的拓扑、皮肤效应、光学响应和动力学提供了新的理解，并提出了一种光子学实现这些现象的可行方案。

Abstract: We propose a non-Hermitian Floquet photonic lattice formed by a physical resonator coordinate and a synthetic frequency coordinate. A two-step modulation protocol realizes a chiral walk in this physical-synthetic plane, with a real synthetic flux controlling loop interference and imaginary gauge fields controlling non-reciprocal envelopes. We show that anomalous corner pairs at quasienergies zero and \(π/T\) exhibit three distinct layers of physics. A non-Bloch higher-order construction predicts whether the \(0/π\) corner pair exists under open boundaries. The imaginary gauge fields select where the right eigenmodes accumulate. The real flux controls the local interference matrix element that determines whether the doubled-period optical response is visible. As a result, the same topological coexistence sector can be bright, skin-dark, or flux-dark in a local optical measurement. We further show that the complex gauge can tune an exceptional point of the two-period corner propagator. At this point the anomalous response keeps its doubled-period sign alternation, but its envelope becomes algebraic because of a Jordan block. These results provide a photonic route to separate topological existence, skin-selected localization, optical visibility, and defective two-period dynamics in a non-Hermitian synthetic dimension.

### [2] [Coherent control of chirality in Weyl semimetals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07921v1)
*Amar Bharti,Margarita Khokhlova,Misha Ivanov,Gopal Dixit*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 本文研究了在无对称性Weyl半金属中，如何通过双色光场的选择性激发打破手性对称性，实现可控的光电流。方法上利用圆偏振和线偏振光的干涉效应，结果显示光电流的大小和方向可通过相位和强度调节，并发现了最佳选择性激发条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在Weyl半金属中，由于手性对称性，圆偏振光激发会导致对称的光学响应和净光电流为零。本文旨在通过设计双色光场打破这种对称性，实现对单个Weyl节点的选择性激发和可控光电流的生成。

Method: 通过结合圆偏振的ω光场和相位锁定的线偏振2ω光场，利用它们的干涉效应，在手性依赖的动量空间中重新分布载流子，从而产生可控的非零光电流。实验和理论分析了相位和场强对光电流的影响。

Result: 研究发现光电流的大小和方向可通过双色光场的相对相位和强度精确调控，并确定了最大化手性选择性的最佳参数范围。

Conclusion: 本文确立了一种利用偏振结构光在Weyl半金属中实现光学控制手性电荷动力学的通用方法，为相关领域的应用提供了新思路。

Abstract: Weyl fermions in inversion-symmetric Weyl semimetals occur in pairs of opposite chirality, leading to symmetric optical responses under circularly-polarised light and a vanishing net photocurrent. Here, we show that tailored two-colour light fields break this symmetry and enable selective excitation of individual Weyl nodes. The interference between a circularly-polarised $ω$ field and a phase-locked linearly-polarised $2ω$ field generates a chirality-dependent redistribution of carriers in momentum space, resulting in a nonzero controllable photocurrent. We demonstrate that both the magnitude and sign of the photocurrent can be tuned via the relative phase and field strength of the two colours, and identify an optimal regime in which chiral selectivity is maximised. Our results establish a general route to optically-controlled chiral charge dynamics in Weyl semimetals using polarisation-structured light.

### [3] [CellSense: A Sub-6 GHz Cellular ISAC System for Clutter-Robust Passive Sensing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07900v1)
*Bibhor Kumar,Ish Kumar Jain,Vijay K Shah*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种名为CellSense的新型sub-6 GHz ISAC架构，集成于5G蜂窝协议栈中，旨在解决现有sub-6 GHz频段中感知能力未充分开发的问题，并通过仿真和硬件实验验证了其在目标跟踪和环境感知中的有效性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 未来无线网络需要超越传统通信的能力，推动集成了环境感知、定位和跟踪的ISAC技术的发展。然而，现有的sub-6 GHz研究过于偏重通信，忽视了感知能力，因此作者试图填补这一研究空白。

Method: 论文引入了CellSense架构，将其原生集成到5G蜂窝协议栈中，利用Sionna基础的OFDM链路级仿真和基于USRP硬件的OpenAirInterface栈原型进行验证，分析了通信与感知之间的权衡，尤其是导频符号密度对吞吐量和感知精度的影响。

Result: 仿真结果显示，CellSense在室内仓库环境中实现了74%的检测概率和1.43米的定位误差，在室外环境（NCSU Centennial校区的Oval区域）提升至94%的检测概率和0.33米的亚米级误差。硬件实验在高杂波的室内实验室中验证了1.28米的定位精度和76%的检测概率。

Conclusion: CellSense架构在sub-6 GHz频段中实现了通信与感知的高效融合，为实际ISAC部署提供了有效解决方案，显著提升了目标跟踪和环境感知的性能。

Abstract: Future wireless networks demand capabilities beyond traditional communication, driving the development of Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) for environmental awareness, localization, and tracking. Ubiquitous cellular deployment allows ISAC to maximize spectral efficiency, lower costs, and expand sensing coverage. However, sub-6 GHz research has heavily favored communication, leaving sensing capabilities largely underexplored. To bridge this gap, we introduce CellSense, a novel sub-6 GHz ISAC architecture natively integrated into the 5G cellular protocol stack for real-world target tracking. We validate the system via Sionna-based orthogonal frequency-division multiplexing (OFDM) link-level simulations and an experimental USRP hardware prototype using the OpenAirInterface (OAI) stack. Furthermore, we analyze the communication-sensing tradeoff by quantifying how pilot symbol density impacts throughput versus sensing accuracy. Simulations show that CellSense achieves a 74 percent detection probability with a 1.43 m localization error in indoor warehouse environment, which improves to 94 percent detection and a sub-meter error of 0.33 m in the outdoor environment of Oval area at the NCSU Centennial campus. Hardware experiments in a highly cluttered indoor laboratory confirm a 1.28 m localization accuracy and 76 percent detection probability, proving its efficacy for practical ISAC deployments.

### [4] [A Double Proportionate Sparse Adaptive Filter for Impulsive Noise Environments](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.08225v1)
*Mohammad Salman,Falah Ibrahim Al Hassan,Hadi Zayyani,Felipe A. P. de Figueiredo,Hasan Abu Hilal*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 这篇论文提出了一种双比例稀疏自适应滤波器（DP-SAF），通过结合稀疏性和抗脉冲噪声的特性，填补了现有技术的空白。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 目前稀疏自适应滤波器和抗脉冲噪声算法通常是独立发展的，而实际应用中往往需要同时具备这两种特性。作者旨在设计一种能够同时实现稀疏性和抗脉冲噪声鲁棒性的自适应滤波器。

Method: 论文提出了一种双比例稀疏自适应滤波器（DP-SAF），其核心是引入两个独立的对角增益矩阵：一个用于根据系数大小按比例调整自适应步长；另一个用于施加与幅度相关的零吸引力。此外，采用符号误差更新来增强抗脉冲噪声的能力。这两种增益矩阵均通过最小范数优化框架导出。

Result: 在伯努利脉冲噪声模型下的仿真结果表明，DP-SAF在稳态均方偏差（MSD）方面优于竞争算法，同时在收敛速度上也表现相当或更优。

Conclusion: DP-SAF成功地结合了稀疏性和抗脉冲噪声的特性，提供了一种高效且鲁棒的解决方案，填补了现有技术的空白。

Abstract: Sparse adaptive filters and impulsive noise robust algorithms have largely been developed along separate tracks, leaving a gap when both properties are needed simultaneously. This letter proposes the double proportionate sparse adaptive filter (DP-SAF), which closes this gap within a single $\mathcal{O}(M)$ update. Two independent diagonal gain matrices are introduced; one scales the adaptation step proportionately to coefficient magnitudes, and the other applies a magnitude-dependent zero-attraction that is strongest for inactive taps. A sign-error update provides robustness against impulsive corruptions. Both gain matrices are derived from a minimum-norm optimization framework. Simulations under a Bernoulli impulsive noise model show that DP-SAF consistently achieves a better steady-state MSD than the competing algorithms while matching or exceeding their convergence speeds.

</details>
